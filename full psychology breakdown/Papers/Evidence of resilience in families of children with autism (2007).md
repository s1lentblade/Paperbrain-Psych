# Evidence of resilience in families of children with autism

**Authors:** Mojdeh Bayat
**Year:** 2007
**Citations:** 501
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1365-2788.2007.00960.x

## Abstract

This study presents evidence that a considerable number of families of children with autism display factors of resilience--reporting having become stronger as a result of disability in the family.

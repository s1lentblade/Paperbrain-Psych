# Early reading acquisition and its relation to reading experience and ability 10 years later.

**Authors:** Anne E. Cunningham, Keith E. Stanovich
**Year:** 1997
**Citations:** 1,177
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037//0012-1649.33.6.934

## Abstract

A group of 1st-graders who were administered a battery of reading tasks in a previous study were followed up as 11th graders. Ten years later, they were administered measures of exposure to print, reading comprehension, vocabulary, and general knowledge. First-grade reading ability was a strong predictor of all of the 11th-grade outcomes and remained so even when measures of cognitive ability were partialed out.

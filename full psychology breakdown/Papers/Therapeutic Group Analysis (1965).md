## Abstract

This is an excellent, clearly conceptualized, and well-written book on group therapy. Dr. Foulkes is a well-trained psychoanalyst, who has maintained his analytic identity. Though an expert on groups, he continues to support the value of individual analysis in transference neuroses. He compares and contrasts individual and group analysis in an unusually clear, accurate, and enlightening fashion. He maintains that the group situation is the most powerful therapeutic agency available, and that group analysis can be as intensive as individual analysis.

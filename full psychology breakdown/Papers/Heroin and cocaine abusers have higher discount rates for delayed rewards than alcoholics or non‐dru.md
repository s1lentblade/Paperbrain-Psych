# Heroin and cocaine abusers have higher discount rates for delayed rewards than alcoholics or non‐drug‐using controls

**Authors:** Kris N. Kirby, Nancy M. Petry
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 718
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1360-0443.2003.00669.x

## Abstract

These data suggest that discount rates vary with the preferred drug of abuse, and that high discount rates should be considered in the development of substance abuse prevention and treatment efforts.

## Abstract

"Now completely revised (over 90% new), this handbook offers the authoritative presentation of theories, methods, and applications in the dynamic field of cultural psychology. Leading scholars review state-of-the-art empirical research on how culture affects nearly every aspect of human functioning. The volume examines how topics fundamental to psychology...such as cognition, emotion, motivation, development, and mental health...are influenced by cultural meanings and practices. It also addresses the psychological and evolutionary underpinnings of cultural stability and change.

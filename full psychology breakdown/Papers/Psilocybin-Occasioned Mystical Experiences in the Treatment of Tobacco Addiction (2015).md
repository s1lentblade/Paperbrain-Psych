# Psilocybin-Occasioned Mystical Experiences in the Treatment of Tobacco Addiction

**Authors:** Albert Garcia‐Romeu, Matthew W. Johnson
**Year:** 2015
**Citations:** 471
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2174/1874473708666150107121331

## Abstract

Psilocybin-occasioned mystical experiences have been linked to persisting effects in healthy volunteers including positive changes in behavior, attitudes, and values, and increases in the personality domain of openness. In an open-label pilot-study of psilocybin-facilitated smoking addiction treatment, 15 smokers received 2 or 3 doses of psilocybin in the context of cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) for smoking cessation. Twelve of 15 participants (80%) demonstrated biologically verified smoking abstinence at 6-month follow-up.

# The relation between emotional intelligence and job performance: A meta‐analysis

**Authors:** Ernest H. O’Boyle, Paul A. Story
**Year:** 2010
**Citations:** 1,075
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/job.714

## Abstract

Abstract This meta‐analysis builds upon a previous meta‐analysis by (1) including 65 per cent more studies that have over twice the sample size to estimate the relationships between emotional intelligence (EI) and job performance; (2) using more current meta‐analytical studies for estimates of relationships among personality variables and for cognitive ability and job performance; (3) using the three‐stream approach for classifying EI research; (4) performing tests for differences among streams of EI research and their relationships with personality and cognitive intelligence; (5) using latest statistical procedures such as dominance analysis; and (6) testing for publication bias.

# A Study of Spirituality in the Workplace

**Authors:** Ian I. Mitroff, Élizabeth Denton
**Year:** 1999
**Citations:** 819

## Abstract

What do managers and executives believe and feel about workplace spirituality or assessments of its purported benefits? In this article, authors present results of a two-year empirical study based on face-to-face interviews and questionnaires. Participants differentiated strongly between religion and spirituality, viewing religion as a highly inappropriate form of expression and spirituality as a highly appropriate subject for workplace. Most believed strongly that organizations must harness immense spiritual energy within each person in order to produce world-class products and services.

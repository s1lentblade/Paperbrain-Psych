# The genetic epidemiology of bulimia nervosa

**Authors:** Kenneth S. Kendler, Lindon J. Eaves
**Year:** 1991
**Citations:** 638
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/ajp.148.12.1627

## Abstract

The liability to fully syndromal bulimia nervosa, which affects around one in 25 women at some point in their lives, is substantially influenced by both epidemiologic and genetic risk factors. The same factors that influence the risk for narrowly defined bulimia also influence the risk for less severe bulimia-like syndromes.

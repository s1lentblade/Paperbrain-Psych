# The public's view of the competence, dangerousness, and need for legal coercion of persons with mental health problems.

**Authors:** Bernice A. Pescosolido, Saeko Kikuzawa
**Year:** 1999
**Citations:** 610
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2105/ajph.89.9.1339

## Abstract

Americans report greater concern with individuals who have drug or alcohol problems than with persons who have other mental health problems. Evaluations of dangerousness and coercion indicate a continuing need for public education.

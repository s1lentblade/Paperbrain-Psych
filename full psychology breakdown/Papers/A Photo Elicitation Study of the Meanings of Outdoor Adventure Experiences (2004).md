# A Photo Elicitation Study of the Meanings of Outdoor Adventure Experiences

**Authors:** TA Loeffler
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 218
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/00222216.2004.11950035

## Abstract

AbstractThis study explored the meanings of participating in college-based outdoor programs utilizing an innovative research methodology. Individual photo elicitation interviews were conducted with 14 participants of a college-based outdoor program. Photographs taken by the participants during their outdoor experiences formed the foundation of the photo elicitation interview process. Photo elicitation provided a model for collaborative research where participants interpreted their photographic images and meanings for the researcher.

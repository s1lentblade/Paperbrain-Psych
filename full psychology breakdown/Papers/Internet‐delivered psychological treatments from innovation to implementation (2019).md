# Internet‐delivered psychological treatments: from innovation to implementation

**Authors:** Gerhard Andersson, Per Carlbring
**Year:** 2019
**Citations:** 699
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/wps.20610

## Abstract

Internet interventions, and in particular Internet-delivered cognitive behaviour therapy (ICBT), have existed for at least 20 years. Here we review the treatment approach and the evidence base, arguing that ICBT can be viewed as a vehicle for innovation. ICBT has been developed and tested for several psychiatric and somatic conditions, and direct comparative studies suggest that therapist-guided ICBT is more effective than a waiting list for anxiety disorders and depression, and tends to be as effective as face-to-face CBT.

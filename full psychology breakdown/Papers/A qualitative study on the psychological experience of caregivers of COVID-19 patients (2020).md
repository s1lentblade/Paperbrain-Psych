# A qualitative study on the psychological experience of caregivers of COVID-19 patients

**Authors:** Niuniu Sun, Hongyun Wang
**Year:** 2020
**Citations:** 1,304
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ajic.2020.03.018

## Abstract

During an epidemic outbreak, positive and negative emotions of the front-line nurses interweaved and coexisted. In the early stage, negative emotions were dominant and positive emotions appeared gradually. Self-coping styles and psychological growth played an important role in maintaining mental health of nurses.

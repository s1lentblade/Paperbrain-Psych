# School Closures During Social Lockdown and Mental Health, Health Behaviors, and Well-being Among Children and Adolescents During the First COVID-19 Wave

**Authors:** Russell Viner, Silvia Minozzi
**Year:** 2022
**Citations:** 646
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/jamapediatrics.2021.5840

## Abstract

In this narrative synthesis of reports from the first wave of the COVID-19 pandemic, studies of short-term school closures as part of social lockdown measures reported adverse mental health symptoms and health behaviors among children and adolescents. Associations between school closure and health outcomes and behaviors could not be separated from broader lockdown measures.

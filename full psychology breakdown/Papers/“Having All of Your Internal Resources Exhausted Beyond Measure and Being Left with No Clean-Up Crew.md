## Abstract

<b>Why was this study done?:</b> Autistic burnout is talked about a lot by autistic people but has not been formally addressed by researchers. It is an important issue for the autistic community because it is described as leading to distress; loss of work, school, health, and quality of life; and even suicidal behavior.<b>What was the purpose of this study?:</b> This study aimed to characterize autistic burnout, understand what it is like, what people think causes it, and what helps people recover from or prevent it.

# Phenomenology as a method to investigate the experience lived: a perspective from Husserl and Merleau Ponty's thought

**Authors:** Maria Lúcia Araújo Sadala, Rubens de Camargo Ferreira Adorno
**Year:** 2002
**Citations:** 332
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1046/j.1365-2648.2002.02071.x

## Abstract

Steps are proposed for such an approach, namely phenomenological description, reduction and analysis. These lead to the building up of ideographic and nomothetic analyses, thus unveiling and describing general truths about the phenomenon studied. Finally, the possibilities for applying the methodology to nursing research are discussed, illustrated by my research into student nurses' perspectives on working on an isolation ward.

# Suicide Risk in Relation to Psychiatric Hospitalization

**Authors:** Ping Qin, Merete Nordentoft
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 615
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/archpsyc.62.4.427

## Abstract

Suicide risk peaks in periods immediately after admission and discharge. The risk is particularly high in persons with affective disorders and in persons with short hospital treatment. These findings should lead to systematic evaluation of suicide risk among inpatients before discharge and corresponding outpatient treatment, and family support should be initiated immediately after the discharge.

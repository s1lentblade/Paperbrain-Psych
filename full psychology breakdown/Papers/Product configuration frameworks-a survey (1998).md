# Product configuration frameworks-a survey

**Authors:** D.D. Sabin, Randolph R. Weigel
**Year:** 1998
**Citations:** 461
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1109/5254.708432

## Abstract

The manufacturing trend toward mass customization has awakened a great interest in automatic product configuration techniques. The authors review the literature on this topic, discussing the most representative knowledge-based approaches.

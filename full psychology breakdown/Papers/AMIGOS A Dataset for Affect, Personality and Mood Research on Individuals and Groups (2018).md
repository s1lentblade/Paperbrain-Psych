## Abstract

We present AMIGOS- A dataset for Multimodal research of affect, personality traits and mood on Individuals and GrOupS. Different to other databases, we elicited affect using both short and long videos in two social contexts, one with individual viewers and one with groups of viewers. The database allows the multimodal study of the affective responses, by means of neuro-physiological signals of individuals in relation to their personality and mood, and with respect to the social context and videos' duration.

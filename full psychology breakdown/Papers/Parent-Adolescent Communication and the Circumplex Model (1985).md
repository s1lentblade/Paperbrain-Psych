BARNES, HOWARD L., and OLSON, DAVID H. Parent-Adolescent Communication and the Circumplex Model. CHILD DEVELOPMENT, 1985, 56, 438-447. This study tested the relationship between parentadolescent communication and the Circumplex Model of Marital and Family Systems. While most studies of the Circumplex Model have focused on problem families, this study used fathers, mothers, and adolescents from 426 normal families. Family scores were developed and used to help describe the type of family system.

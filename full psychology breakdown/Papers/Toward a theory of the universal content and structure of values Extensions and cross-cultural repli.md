# Toward a theory of the universal content and structure of values: Extensions and cross-cultural replications.

**Authors:** Shalom H. Schwartz, Wolfgang Bilsky
**Year:** 1990
**Citations:** 2,202
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/0022-3514.58.5.878

## Abstract

The universality of Schwartz and Bilsky's (1987) theory of the psychological content and structure of human values was examined with data from Australia, Finland, Hong Kong, Spain, and the United States

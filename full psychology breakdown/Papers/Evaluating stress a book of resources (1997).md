# Evaluating stress : a book of resources

**Authors:** Carlos P. Zalaquett, Richard James Wood
**Year:** 1997
**Citations:** 1,179

## Abstract

Wood and Zalaquett have produced the first resource that brings together accurate and detailed descriptions of instruments designed to measure stress. The authors identified renowned experts in various therapeutic and stress management fields and asked them to present information about the instruments they have developed. The result is this compendium that describes 21 different instruments that are designed to measure a broad range of factors.

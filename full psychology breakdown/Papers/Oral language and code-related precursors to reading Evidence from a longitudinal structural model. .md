**Year:** 2002
**Citations:** 1,615
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/0012-1649.38.6.934

## Abstract

This study examined code-related and oral language precursors to reading in a longitudinal study of 626 children from preschool through 4th grade. Code-related precursors, including print concepts and phonological awareness, and oral language were assessed in preschool and kindergarten. Reading accuracy and reading comprehension skills were examined in 1st through 4th grades.

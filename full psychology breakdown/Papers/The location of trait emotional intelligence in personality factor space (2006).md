# The location of trait emotional intelligence in personality factor space

**Authors:** K. V. Petrides, Flora Kokkinaki
**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 1,347
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1348/000712606x120618

## Abstract

The construct of trait emotional intelligence (trait EI or trait emotional self-efficacy) provides a comprehensive operationalization of emotion-related self-perceptions and dispositions. In the first part of the present study (N=274, 92 males), we performed two joint factor analyses to determine the location of trait EI in Eysenckian and Big Five factor space. The results showed that trait EI is a compound personality construct located at the lower levels of the two taxonomies. In the second part of the study, we performed six two-step hierarchical regressions to investigate the incremental validity of trait EI in predicting, over and above the Giant Three and Big Five personality dimension

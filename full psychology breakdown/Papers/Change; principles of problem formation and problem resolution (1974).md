# Change; principles of problem formation and problem resolution

**Authors:** Paul Watzlawick, Richard Fisch
**Year:** 1974
**Citations:** 1,698

## Abstract

This classic book, available in paperback for the very first time, explores why some people can successfully change their lives and others cannot. Here famed psychologist Paul Watzlawick presents what is still often perceived as a radical idea: that the solutions to our problems are inherently embedded in the problems themselves. Tackling the age-old questions surrounding persistence and change, the book asks why problems arise and are perpetuated in some instances but easily resolved in others.

# Evaluating Iatrogenic Risk of Youth Suicide Screening Programs

**Authors:** Madelyn S. Gould, Mark Davies
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 519
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/jama.293.13.1635

## Abstract

No evidence of iatrogenic effects of suicide screening emerged. Screening in high schools is a safe component of youth suicide prevention efforts.

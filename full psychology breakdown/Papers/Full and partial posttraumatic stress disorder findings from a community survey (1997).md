# Full and partial posttraumatic stress disorder: findings from a community survey

**Authors:** Murray B. Stein, David R. Forde
**Year:** 1997
**Citations:** 1,017
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/ajp.154.8.1114

## Abstract

These findings in an epidemiologic sample underscore observations from patient and military groups that many traumatized persons suffer from a subsyndromal form of PTSD. These persons with partial PTSD, although somewhat less impaired than persons with the full syndrome, nonetheless exhibit clinically meaningful levels of functional impairment in association with their symptoms. This subthreshold form of PTSD may be especially prevalent in women. Additional study of partial PTSD is warranted.

# A Revised Paranormal Belief Scale

**Authors:** Jerome Tobacyk
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 492
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.24972/ijts.2004.23.1.94

## Abstract

A 26-item Revised Paranormal Belief Scale is introduced which provides a measure of degree of\nbelief in each of seven dimensions: Traditional Religious Belief, Psi, Witchcraft, Superstition,\nSpiritualism, Extraordinary Life Forms, and Precognition. Improvements from the original 25-\nitem Paranormal Belief Scale (Tobacyk &amp; Milford, 1983) include adoption of a seven-point rating\nscale as well as item changes for three subscales: Precognition, Witchcraft, and Extraordinary\nLife Forms. These improvements provide greater reliability and validity, less restriction of range,\nand greater cross-cultural validity.

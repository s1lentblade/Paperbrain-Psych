# Are Mood Disorders and Obesity Related? A Review for the Mental Health Professional

**Authors:** Susan L. McElroy, Charles B. Nemeroff
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 655
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.4088/jcp.v65n0507

## Abstract

Although the overlap between mood disorders and obesity may be coincidental, it suggests the two conditions may be related. Clinical and theoretical implications of this overlap are discussed, and further research is called for.

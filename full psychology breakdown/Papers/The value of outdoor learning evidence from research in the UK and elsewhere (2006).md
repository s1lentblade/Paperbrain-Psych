**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 479

## Abstract

This article summarises the key findings of a review of the research into outdoor learning published between 1993 and 2003. It summarises what is known about the impacts of fieldwork and then discusses what is known about effective practice before concluding with a look at barriers to fieldwork. This article summarises the key findings of a review that critically examined 150 pieces of research on outdoor learning published between 1993 and 2003 (Rickinson et al. , 2004).

# Suicide Risk in Relation to Socioeconomic, Demographic, Psychiatric, and Familial Factors: A National Register–Based Study of All Suicides in Denmark, 1981–1997

**Authors:** Ping Qin, Preben Bo Mortensen
**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 960
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/appi.ajp.160.4.765

## Abstract

Suicide risk is strongly associated with mental illness, unemployment, low income, marital status, and family history of suicide. The effect of most risk factors differs significantly by gender.

**Authors:** Louis Menand
**Year:** 2001
**Citations:** 444

## Abstract

Following the Civil War, "it took nearly half a century for Americans to develop a set of ideas, a way of thinking, that would help them cope with the conditions of modern life. That struggle is the subject of this book."--Jacket.

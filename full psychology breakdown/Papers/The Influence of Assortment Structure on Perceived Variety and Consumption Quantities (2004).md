# The Influence of Assortment Structure on Perceived Variety and Consumption Quantities

**Authors:** Barbara E. Kahn, Brian Wansink
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 617
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1086/380286

## Abstract

Increasing the actual variety of an assortment has been shown previously to increase the quantity consumed. We show, however, that consumption quantities are also influenced by the perceived variety of an assortment. In combination, six lab and field studies show that the structure of an assortment (e.g., organization and symmetry or entropy) moderates the effect of actual variety on perceived variety. We further show that it is perceived variety that in turn influences consumption quantities through anticipated consumption utility.

**Authors:** Paul Gilbert, Steven Allan
**Year:** 1998
**Citations:** 944
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1017/s0033291798006710

## Abstract

Defeat and entrapment appear to be promising variables for the study of depression. These variables may also help to develop linkages between human and animal models of psychopathology.

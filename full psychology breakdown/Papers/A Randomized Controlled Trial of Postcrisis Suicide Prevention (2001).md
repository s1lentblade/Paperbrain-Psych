# A Randomized Controlled Trial of Postcrisis Suicide Prevention

**Authors:** Jerome A. Motto, Alan Bostrom
**Year:** 2001
**Citations:** 517
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/appi.ps.52.6.828

## Abstract

A systematic program of contact with persons who are at risk of suicide and who refuse to remain in the health care system appears to exert a significant preventive influence for at least two years. Diminution of the frequency of contact and discontinuation of contact appear to reduce and eventually eliminate this preventive influence.

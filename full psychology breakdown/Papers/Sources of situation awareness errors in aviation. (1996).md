# Sources of situation awareness errors in aviation.

**Authors:** D.G. Jones, Mica R. Endsley
**Year:** 1996
**Citations:** 415

## Abstract

These results give an indication of the types and frequency of SA errors that occur in aviation, with failure to monitor or observe available information forming the largest single category. Many other causal factors are also indicated, however, including vigilance, automation problems, and poor mental models.

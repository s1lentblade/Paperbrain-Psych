# Measuring empowerment in families whose children have emotional disabilities: A brief questionnaire.

**Authors:** Paul E. Koren, Barbara J. Friesen
**Year:** 1992
**Citations:** 670
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/h0079106

## Abstract

This paper describes the development and empirical examination of a brief questionnaire for assessing empowerment in families whose children have emotional disabilities. The questionnaire is based on a two-dimensional conceptual framework of empowerment derived from the literature. One dimension reflects empowerment with respect to the family, service system, and larger community and political environment; the other dimension reflects the expression of empowerment as attitudes, knowledge, and behaviors.

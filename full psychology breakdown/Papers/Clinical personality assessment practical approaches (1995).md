# Clinical personality assessment : practical approaches

**Authors:** James N. Butcher
**Year:** 1995
**Citations:** 317

## Abstract

In this book, the second in the Oxford Textbooks in Clinical Psychology series, 30 eminent contributors (from diverse psychological disciplines) address central issues in assessment. Topics include ethical considerations in clinical personality assessment, assessment of ethnic and racial minorities, and assessment of the elderly, for example. The book maintains a practical, context-based approach throughout, and will appeal to both graduate students and practising clinical psychologists.

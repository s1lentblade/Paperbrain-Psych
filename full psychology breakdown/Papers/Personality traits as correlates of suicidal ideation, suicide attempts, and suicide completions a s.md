# Personality traits as correlates of suicidal ideation, suicide attempts, and suicide completions: a systematic review

**Authors:** Jelena Brezo, Gustavo Turecki
**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 595
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1600-0447.2005.00702.x

## Abstract

Selected personality traits may be useful markers of suicide risk. Future research needs to establish their contributions in relation to environmental and genetic variation in different gender, age, and ethnocultural groups.

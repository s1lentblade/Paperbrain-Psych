**Citations:** 224

## Abstract

OBJECTIVE: To investigate the relative effects of two experimental interventions on the use of physical restraints. DESIGN: Prospective 12‐month clinical trial in which three nursing homes were randomly assigned to restraint education (RE), restraint education‐with‐consultation (REC), or control (C). SETTING: Three voluntary nursing homes in the Philadelphia area providing both skilled and intermediate care.

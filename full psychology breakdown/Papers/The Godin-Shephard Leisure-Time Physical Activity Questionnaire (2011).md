# The Godin-Shephard Leisure-Time Physical Activity Questionnaire

**Authors:** Gaston Godin
**Year:** 2011
**Citations:** 909
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.14288/hfjc.v4i1.82

## Abstract

The aim of this paper is to overview the development process of the Godin and Shephard leisuretime physical activity questionnaire, to present the questionnaire and how to compute an overall score as well as a score related to health benefits.

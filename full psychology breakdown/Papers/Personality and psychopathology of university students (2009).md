# Personality and psychopathology of university students

**Authors:** Dušica Lečić Toševski, Saveta Draganic Gajic
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 259
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1097/yco.0b013e328333d625

## Abstract

Acquiring and improving knowledge about the student population is a crucial factor in the development of mental health promotion programs in order to meet their needs and to help them cope with various problems. Better understanding and care of the personality profile of university students can be helpful in academic and career choice and prevention of future mental health problems.

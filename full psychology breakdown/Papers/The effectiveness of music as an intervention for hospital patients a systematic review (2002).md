# The effectiveness of music as an intervention for hospital patients: a systematic review

**Authors:** David Evans
**Year:** 2002
**Citations:** 361
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1046/j.1365-2648.2002.02052.x

## Abstract

This review demonstrates the effectiveness of music for the reduction of anxiety during normal care deliver. Given the inexpensive nature of this intervention, and the lack of adverse events, it is recommended as an adjunct to normal care practices. This review also highlights the need for further research into many aspect of this intervention.

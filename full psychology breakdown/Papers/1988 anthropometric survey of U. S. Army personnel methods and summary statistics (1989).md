# 1988 anthropometric survey of U. S. Army personnel : methods and summary statistics

**Authors:** Claire C. Gordon, John T. McConville
**Year:** 1989
**Citations:** 416

## Abstract

Results of the 1987-1988 anthropometric survey of Army personnel are presented in this report in the form of summary statistics, percentile data and frequency distribution. These anthropometric data are presented for a subset of personnel (1774 men and 2208 women) sampled to match the proportions of age categories and racial/ethnic groups found in the active duty Army of June 1988.

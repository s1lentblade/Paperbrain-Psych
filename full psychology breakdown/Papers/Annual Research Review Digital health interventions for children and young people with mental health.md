# Annual Research Review: Digital health interventions for children and young people with mental health problems – a systematic and meta‐review

**Authors:** Chris Hollis, E Bethan Davies
**Year:** 2016
**Citations:** 873
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/jcpp.12663

## Abstract

Key methodological limitations make it difficult to draw definitive conclusions from existing clinical trials of DHIs. Issues include variable uptake and engagement with DHIs, lack of an agreed typology/taxonomy for DHIs, small sample sizes, lack of blinded outcome assessment, combining different comparators, short-term follow-up and poor specification of the level of human support. Research and practice recommendations are presented that address the key research questions and methodological issues for the evaluation and clinical implementation of DHIs for CYP.

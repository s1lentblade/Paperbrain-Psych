# Foundations of Human Resource Development

**Year:** 2014
**Citations:** 730
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.4135/9781473906891.n1

## Abstract

Foundations of Human Resource Development is a careful presentation of the basic theory and practice of human resource development. The book clearly frames and explains HRD in a manner that is useful for HRD beginners and experts. Working definitions and core values derived from the history of HRD and its present challenges are presented. In addition, the complimentary learning and performance perspectives in the profession are threaded throughout the book.

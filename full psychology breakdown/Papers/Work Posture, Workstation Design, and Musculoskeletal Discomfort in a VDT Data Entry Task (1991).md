# Work Posture, Workstation Design, and Musculoskeletal Discomfort in a VDT Data Entry Task

**Authors:** Steven L. Sauter, Sheri J. Knutson
**Year:** 1991
**Citations:** 320
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1177/001872089103300203

## Abstract

Self-report data on musculoskeletal discomfort were collected from several hundred VDT users in two agencies of a state government. Aspects of worker posture and workstation design were objectively assessed for 40 of the VDT users. Multiple regression analyses were used to examine the relationship between these ergonomic variables and musculoskeletal discomfort. Effects of ergonomic factors on musculoskeletal discomfort were clearly evident in the analyses. Regression models explained up to 38% of the variance in discomfort at different body sites.

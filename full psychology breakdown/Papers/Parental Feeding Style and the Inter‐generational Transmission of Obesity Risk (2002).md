# Parental Feeding Style and the Inter‐generational Transmission of Obesity Risk

**Authors:** Jane Wardle, Robert Plomin
**Year:** 2002
**Citations:** 539
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1038/oby.2002.63

## Abstract

These results suggest that the stereotype of the obese mother, who uses food in nonnutritive ways so that her child also becomes obese, is more likely to be myth than fact. However, the results raise the possibility that lack of control of food intake might contribute to the emergence of differences in weight.

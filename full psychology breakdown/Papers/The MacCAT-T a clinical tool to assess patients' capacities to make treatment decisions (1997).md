# The MacCAT-T: a clinical tool to assess patients' capacities to make treatment decisions

**Authors:** Thomas Grisso, Carolyn Hill‐Fotouhi
**Year:** 1997
**Citations:** 685
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/ps.48.11.1415

## Abstract

The MacCAT-T offers a flexible yet structured method with which caregivers can assess, rate, and report patients' abilities relevant for evaluating competence to consent to treatment.

# The Origins of Sex Differences in Human Behavior: Evolved Dispositions versus Social Roles

**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 1,091
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.7551/mitpress/2874.003.0015

## Abstract

The origins of sex differences in human behavior can lie mainly in evolved dispositions that differ by sex or mainly in the differing placement of women and men in the social structure. The present article contrasts these 2 origin theories of sex differences and illustrates the explanatory power of each to account for the overall differences between the mate selection preferences of men and women.

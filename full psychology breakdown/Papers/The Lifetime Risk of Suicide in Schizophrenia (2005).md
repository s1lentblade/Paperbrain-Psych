**Authors:** Brian A. Palmer, John Bostwick
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 999
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/archpsyc.62.3.247

## Abstract

This study estimates that 4.9% of schizophrenics will commit suicide during their lifetimes, usually near illness onset.

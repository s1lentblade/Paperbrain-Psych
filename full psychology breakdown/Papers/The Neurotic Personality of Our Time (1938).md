## Abstract

The interested layman as well as the physician will find here a form of psychologic analysis built on the freudian contribution but not essentially the same. Dr. Horney recognizes the great influence of our environment and culture on our points of view and on our mentality. Perhaps the most significant of the chapters in the work is the consideration given to the part played by sexuality in the neurotic need for affection.

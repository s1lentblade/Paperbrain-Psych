# Language, culture and mind

**Authors:** Michel Achard, Suzanne Kemmer
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 589

## Abstract

In this book, Michel Achard and Suzanne Kemmer present thirty-five original essays bringing together work at the crossroads of linguistics, psychology, philosophy, cognitive science, and related fields. These contributions apply a range of methodologies and perspectives to the problem of the relation of language to human culture and cognition, with an emphasis on how language is produced and understood in context.

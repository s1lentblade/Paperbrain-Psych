# The development of a clinician‐administered PTSD scale

**Authors:** Dudley David Blake, Terence M. Keane
**Year:** 1995
**Citations:** 3,659
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/jts.2490080106

## Abstract

Abstract Several interviews are available for assessing PTSD. These interviews vary in merit when compared on stringent psychometric and utility standards. Of all the interviews, the Clinician‐Administered PTSD Scale (CAPS‐1) appears to satisfy these standards most uniformly. The CAPS‐1 is a structured interview for assessing core and associated symptoms of PTSD. It assesses the frequency and intensity of each symptom using standard prompt questions and explicit, behaviorally‐anchored rating scales.

# Reading Dancing: Bodies and Subjects in Contemporary American Dance

**Authors:** Philip Auslander, Susan Leigh Foster
**Year:** 1988
**Citations:** 195
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2307/1145884

## Abstract

Posing the question of dance means what it does, Foster uses post-structuralist criticism to de-naturalize the body, the subject, and the expressive act. She compares the codes and conventions of work used by choreographers throughout the history of dance and looks at recent work that uses a multi-disciplinary approach to examine how the body accrues meaning.

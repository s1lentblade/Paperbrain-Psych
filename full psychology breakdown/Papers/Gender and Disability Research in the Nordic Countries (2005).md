## Abstract

The crystal clarity and jargon-free accessibility of the writing in this anthology make it a joy to read. The conceptual sophistication demonstrated in its articles, and the depth of analysis explored in many of them, mean that this volume advances significantly the fields of disability studies and gender studies worldwide.

**Authors:** K.M. Harris, Denise Reid
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 269
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1177/000841740507200107

## Abstract

The overall volitional scores of children with cerebral palsy in the current study indicate that VR play is a motivating activity and thus has potential as a successful intervention tool.

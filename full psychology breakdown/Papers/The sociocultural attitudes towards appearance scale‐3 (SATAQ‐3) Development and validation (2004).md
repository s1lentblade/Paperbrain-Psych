**Authors:** J. Kevin Thompson, Leslie J. Heinberg
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 1,172
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/eat.10257

## Abstract

The SATAQ-3 measures multiple aspects of a societal influence and should prove useful for basic risk factor work as well as for gauging the efficacy of prevention and treatment programs.

# The Impact of Professionally Conducted Cultural Programs on the Physical Health, Mental Health, and Social Functioning of Older Adults

**Authors:** Gene D. Cohen, Samuel J. Simmens
**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 547
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1093/geront/46.6.726

## Abstract

The positive impact of participatory art programs for older adults in this study on overall health, doctor visits, medication use, falls, loneliness, morale, and activities reflects important health promotion and prevention effects and a reduction of risk factors driving the need for long-term care.

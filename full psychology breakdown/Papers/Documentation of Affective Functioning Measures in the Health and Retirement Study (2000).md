# Documentation of Affective Functioning Measures in the Health and Retirement Study

**Authors:** Diane Steffick
**Year:** 2000
**Citations:** 516
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.7826/isr-um.06.585031.001.05.0005.2000

## Abstract

Section V assesses the comparability of the measures across the HRS waves (1992, 1994, and 1996) and AHEAD waves (1993 and 1995). Section VI provides the results of investigations into the quality of the measures, using data from the first few waves of each survey (HRS/AHEAD). This section included subsections on univariate distributions, benchmarking against other surveys and prevalence measures, internal consistency and measurement properties, constructed variables, item nonresponse and missing data, and construct validity. The Appendix contains additional tables, which may be of interest to the reader, while the Technical Appendix contains the SAS code for constructed summary variables.

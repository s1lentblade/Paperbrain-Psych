# Nonpharmacologic Treatment of Chronic Insomnia

**Authors:** Charles M. Morin, Richard R. Bootzin
**Year:** 1999
**Citations:** 881
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1093/sleep/22.8.1134

## Abstract

This paper reviews the evidence regarding the efficacy of nonpharmacological treatments for primary chronic insomnia. It is based on a review of 48 clinical trials and two meta-analyses conducted by a task force appointed by the American Academy of Sleep Medicine to develop practice parameters on non-drug therapies for the clinical management of insomnia. The findings indicate that nonpharmacological therapies produce reliable and durable changes in several sleep parameters of chronic insomnia sufferers.

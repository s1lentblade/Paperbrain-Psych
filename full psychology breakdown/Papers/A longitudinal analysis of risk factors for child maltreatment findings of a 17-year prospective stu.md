# A longitudinal analysis of risk factors for child maltreatment: findings of a 17-year prospective study of officially recorded and self-reported child abuse and neglect

**Authors:** J. F. Brown, Suzanne Salzinger
**Year:** 1998
**Citations:** 835
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/s0145-2134(98)00087-8

## Abstract

Assessment of a number of risk factors may permit health professionals to identify parents and children who are at high risk for child maltreatment, facilitating appropriate implementation of prevention and treatment interventions.

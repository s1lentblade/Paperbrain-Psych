# Enhancing user engagement: The role of gamification in mobile apps

**Authors:** Paula Bitrián, Sara Catalán
**Year:** 2021
**Citations:** 379
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jbusres.2021.04.028

## Abstract

Organizations are increasingly making use of gamification to enhance users’ engagement with their mobile apps. However, more research into the mechanisms that facilitate user engagement and its consequences is needed. Drawing on the self-system model of motivational development, this study investigates how gamification might foster user engagement and positive marketing outcomes. Data from 276 users of a mobile gamified app were analyzed using partial least squares regression. The results showed that gamification increases user engagement through satisfaction of the needs for competence, autonomy and relatedness.

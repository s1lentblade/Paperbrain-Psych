# Psychiatric Morbidity and Burnout in the Medical Profession: An Italian Study of General Practitioners and Hospital Physicians

**Authors:** Luigi Grassi, Katia Magnani
**Year:** 2000
**Citations:** 196
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1159/000012416

## Abstract

The study underscores the significant problem of stress among physicians and indicates the need for supporting health professionals in order to improve their psychological well-being and, possibly, the quality of their relationship with the patients.

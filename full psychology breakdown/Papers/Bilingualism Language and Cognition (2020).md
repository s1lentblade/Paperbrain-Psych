## Abstract

Bilingualism can be seen everywhere around the globe. There are different reasons for being bilingual for instance, migration, intermarriages, educational, and vocational opportunities. A person who uses at least two languages with some degree of proficiency in both of them is called bilingual. According to Longman Dictionary of Language Teaching and Applied Linguistics bilingual means an individual who can speak, read or understand two languages equally well, but he/she has a better acquaintance of one language than another.

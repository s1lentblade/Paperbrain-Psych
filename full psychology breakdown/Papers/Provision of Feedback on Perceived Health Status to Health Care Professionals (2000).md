# Provision of Feedback on Perceived Health Status to Health Care Professionals

**Authors:** Mireia Espallargues, Jordi Alonso
**Year:** 2000
**Citations:** 210
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1097/00005650-200002000-00007

## Abstract

The provision of feedback on perceived health status to health professionals seems to have an effect on the process of care but not on patient functional or health status. This is especially true with regard to mental health status information. Nevertheless, there is still need for a more through evaluation of this type of intervention.

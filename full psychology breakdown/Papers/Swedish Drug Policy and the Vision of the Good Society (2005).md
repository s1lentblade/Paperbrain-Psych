# Swedish Drug Policy and the Vision of the Good Society

**Authors:** Henrik Tham
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 36
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/14043850510035128

## Abstract

Swedish drug policy has according to official declarations been successful. The picture has recently been challenged through rising drug use and rising drug related mortality. This development has taken place in spite of the restrictive Swedish policy with further penalization of drug consumption, increasing number of police officers working with drug crime and rising number of persons sentenced to prison for drug offences. The question to be explored here is then what strategies the Swedish Government has chosen in the light of the development and how these strategies should be explained.

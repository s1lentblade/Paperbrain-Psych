**Authors:** Hyungshim Jang, Edward L. Deci
**Year:** 2010
**Citations:** 1,575

## Abstract

We investigated 2 engagement-fostering aspects of teachers' instructional styles-autonomy support and structure-and hypothesized that students' engagement would be highest when teachers provided high levels of both. Trained observers rated teachers' instructional styles and students' behavioral engagement in 133 public high school classrooms in the Midwest, and 1,584 students in Grades 9-11 reported their subjective engagement.

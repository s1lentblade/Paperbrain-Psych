# Quality of life in older ages

**Authors:** G. Netuveli, David Blane
**Year:** 2008
**Citations:** 500
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1093/bmb/ldn003

## Abstract

With all other influences controlled, ageing does not influence quality of life negatively; rather a long period of good quality of life is possible. Therefore, the maintenance and improvement quality of life should be included among the goals of clinical management.

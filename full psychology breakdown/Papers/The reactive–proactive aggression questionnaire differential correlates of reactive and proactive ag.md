# The reactive–proactive aggression questionnaire: differential correlates of reactive and proactive aggression in adolescent boys

**Authors:** Adrian Raine, Jianghong Liu
**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 1,614

## Abstract

This study reports the development of the Reactive-Proactive Aggression Questionnaire (RPQ), and the differential correlates of these two forms of aggression. Antisocial, psychosocial and personality measures were obtained at ages 7 and 16 years in schoolboys, while the RPQ was administered to 334 of the boys at age 16 years. Confirmatory factor analysis indicated a significant fit for a two-factor proactive-reactive model that replicated from one independent subsample to another.

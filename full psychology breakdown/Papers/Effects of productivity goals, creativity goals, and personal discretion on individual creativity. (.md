# Effects of productivity goals, creativity goals, and personal discretion on individual creativity.

**Authors:** Christina E. Shalley
**Year:** 1991
**Citations:** 623
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/0021-9010.76.2.179

## Abstract

A three-factor (3 × 3 × 2), between-subjects design was used to investigate effects of productivity goals (difficult, do your best, or no goals), creativity goals (difficult, do your best, or no goals), and personal discretion (high or low) on two dependent variables: creativity and productivity.

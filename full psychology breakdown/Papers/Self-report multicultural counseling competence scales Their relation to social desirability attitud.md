# Self-report multicultural counseling competence scales: Their relation to social desirability attitudes and multicultural case conceptualization ability.

**Authors:** Madonna G. Constantine, Nicholas Ladany
**Year:** 2000
**Citations:** 302
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/0022-0167.47.2.155

## Abstract

This study examined the relationship between 4 self-report multicultural counseling competence measures and a general index of social desirability. The authors also investigated the association between these 4 multicultural scales and multicultural case conceptualization ability. Results revealed significant positive relationships between 3 of the 4 multicultural counseling competence inventories and a measure of social desirability attitudes. Additionally, after controlling for social desirability, none of the self-report multicultural counseling competence scales were significantly related to multicultural case conceptualization ability.

# Association of Torture and Other Potentially Traumatic Events With Mental Health Outcomes Among Populations Exposed to Mass Conflict and Displacement

**Authors:** Zachary Steel, Mark van Ommeren
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 2,172
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/jama.2009.1132

## Abstract

Methodological factors and substantive population risk factors, such as exposure to torture and other PTEs, after adjusting for methodological factors account for higher rates of reported prevalence of PTSD and depression.

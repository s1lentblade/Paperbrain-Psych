# Magnitude Estimation of Linguistic Acceptability

**Authors:** Ellen Gurman Bard, Antonella Sorace
**Year:** 1996
**Citations:** 722

## Abstract

Judgments of linguistic acceptability constitute an important source of evidence for theoretical and applied linguistics, but are typically elicited and represented in ways which limit their utility. This paper describes how MAGNITUDE ESTIMATION, a technique used in psychophysics, can be adapted for eliciting acceptability judgments.

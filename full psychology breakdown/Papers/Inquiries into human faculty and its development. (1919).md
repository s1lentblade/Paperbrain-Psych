# Inquiries into human faculty and its development.

**Authors:** Francis Galton
**Year:** 1919
**Citations:** 1,408
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/10913-000

## Abstract

The first systematic, scientific treatise on individual differences in psychological phenomena. Francis Galton's work marked the beginning of the scientific study of imagery and the association of ideas. Here for the first time he sketched out the essentials of a theory of mental testing.

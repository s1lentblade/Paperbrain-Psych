# Anthropometric measures for better cardiovascular and musculoskeletal health

**Authors:** I Ketut Arta Widana, I Wayan Suryasa
**Year:** 2020
**Citations:** 559
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/cae.22202

## Abstract

Abstract Artists spend their working time in the workplace so a design is needed to address their needs. The workplaces need to be economic, ergonomic, energy‐efficient, and environmentally friendly based on the current trends. This study focused on all of these aspects while choosing the subjects for conducting the experiment. A total of 10 test subjects were involved in this study. Data related to environmental conditions were analyzed by the Mann–Whitney test and those related to workloads and musculoskeletal disorders were tested by two‐paired sample t test.

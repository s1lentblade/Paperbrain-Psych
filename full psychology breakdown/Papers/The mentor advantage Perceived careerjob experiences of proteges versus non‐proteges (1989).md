**Citations:** 610

## Abstract

Abstract Do proteges report having more positive job/career experiences than non‐proteges? Do male proteges report having more favorable job/career experiences than female proteges? Do high‐level proteges report having more favourable work experiences than low‐level proteges? To answer these questions, mentored and non‐mentored men and women in high‐ and low‐level positions were asked to evaluate, on a survey questionnaire, their level of satisfaction, career mobility/opportunity, recognition, security and promotion rate.

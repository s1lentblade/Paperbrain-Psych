# Segmental durations in the vicinity of prosodic phrase boundaries

**Authors:** Colin W. Wightman, Patti Price
**Year:** 1992
**Citations:** 900
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1121/1.402450

## Abstract

Numerous studies have indicated that prosodic phrase boundaries may be marked by a variety of acoustic phenomena including segmental lengthening. It has not been established, however, whether this lengthening is restricted to the immediate vicinity of the boundary, or if it extends over some larger region. In this study, segmental lengthening in the vicinity of prosodic boundaries is examined and found to be restricted to the rhyme of the syllable preceding the boundary.

# Studies on Hysteria

**Authors:** Josef Breuer, Angela Richards
**Year:** 1895
**Citations:** 1,622

## Abstract

Studies on Hysteria (1893 - 1895). This collection of twenty-four volumes is the first full paperback publication of the standard edition of The Complete Psychological Works of Sigmund Freud in English. Includes: On the Pyschical Mechanism of Hysterical Phenomena: A Preliminary Compunction (1893). Case Histories: Anna O., Emmy von N., Lucy R., Katharina, Elisabeth von R. Theoretical Essays (by Josef Breuer). The Psychotherapy of Hysteria.

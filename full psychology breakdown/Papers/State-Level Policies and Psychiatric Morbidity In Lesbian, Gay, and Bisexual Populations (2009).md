# State-Level Policies and Psychiatric Morbidity In Lesbian, Gay, and Bisexual Populations

**Authors:** Mark L. Hatzenbuehler, Deborah S. Hasin
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 534
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2105/ajph.2008.153510

## Abstract

State-level protective policies modify the effect of lesbian, gay, or bisexual status on psychiatric disorders. Policies that reduce discrimination against gays and lesbians are urgently needed to protect the health and well-being of this population.

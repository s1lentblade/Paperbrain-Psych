## Abstract

Science fiction need not be only about alien worlds, HAL-series guidance systems, and starships, but may also focus on such commonplace subjects as the skin. If we wish to understand certain odd but popular schools of sociology and anthropology, it is best to recognize them as commercial journalism, dressed up, like Ashley Montagu's<i>Touching</i>, in new titles and presumptions. Montagu, distinguished as a teacher and successful as an author (more than 60 books), has produced many works that typify this colorful trade.

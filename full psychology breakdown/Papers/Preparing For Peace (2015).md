# Preparing For Peace

**Authors:** John Paul Lederach
**Year:** 2015
**Citations:** 833
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2307/j.ctv10kmc7m

## Abstract

This text explores conflict resolution training and relationship to culture. It blends a special training tradition in mediation with a tradition derived from the author's work in development. Lederach uses anecdotes and experiences to demonstrate his resolution techniques.

## Abstract

Conceived as a deficiency in capacity, feeblemindedness isolates the subject by virtue of that deficiency. In seeking a definite cause of feeblemindedness one is denying that it can have any meaning – that is, a history – or that it may correspond to a situation.(Maud Mannoni, 1972, p. 44)

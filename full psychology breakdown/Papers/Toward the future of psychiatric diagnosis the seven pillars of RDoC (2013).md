# Toward the future of psychiatric diagnosis: the seven pillars of RDoC

**Authors:** Bruce N. Cuthbert, Thomas R. Insel
**Year:** 2013
**Citations:** 3,009
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1186/1741-7015-11-126

## Abstract

Future diagnostic systems cannot reflect ongoing advances in genetics, neuroscience and cognitive science until a literature organized around these disciplines is available to inform the revision efforts. The goal of the RDoC project is to provide a framework for research to transform the approach to the nosology of mental disorders.

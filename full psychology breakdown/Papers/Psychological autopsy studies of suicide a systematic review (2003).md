# Psychological autopsy studies of suicide: a systematic review

**Authors:** Jonathan Cavanagh, S. M. LAWRIE
**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 2,190
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1017/s0033291702006943

## Abstract

The results indicated that mental disorder was the most strongly associated variable of those that have been studied. Further studies should focus on specific disorders and psychosocial factors. Suicide prevention strategies may be most effective if focused on the treatment of mental disorders.

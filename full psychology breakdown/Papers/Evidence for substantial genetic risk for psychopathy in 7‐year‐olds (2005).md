# Evidence for substantial genetic risk for psychopathy in 7‐year‐olds

**Authors:** Essi Viding, Robert Plomin
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 769
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1469-7610.2004.00393.x

## Abstract

The remarkably high heritability for CU, and for AB children with CU, suggests that molecular genetic research on antisocial behaviour should focus on the CU core of psychopathy. Our findings also raise questions for public policy on interventions for antisocial behaviour.

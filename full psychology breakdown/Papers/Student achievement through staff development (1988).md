# Student achievement through staff development

**Authors:** Bruce Joyce, Beverly Showers
**Year:** 1988
**Citations:** 2,117

## Abstract

In this chapter the authors explain how they have developed and refined their vision for training as the means by which new knowledge is added to the teacher’s professional repertoire. They suggest that over the years since they first advocated coaching as an essential ingredient in using new knowledge to change practice in the early 1980s, training designs have come to distinguish more effectively between awareness-raising as a training objective, and behaviour change.

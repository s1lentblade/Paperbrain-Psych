# Marital quality and health: A meta-analytic review.

**Authors:** Theodore F. Robles, Meghan M. McGinn
**Year:** 2013
**Citations:** 1,515
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/a0031859

## Abstract

This meta-analysis reviewed 126 published empirical articles over the past 50 years describing associations between marital relationship quality and physical health in more than 72,000 individuals. Health outcomes included clinical endpoints (objective assessments of function, disease severity, and mortality; subjective health assessments) and surrogate endpoints (biological markers that substitute for clinical endpoints, such as blood pressure). Biological mediators included cardiovascular reactivity and hypothalamic-pituitary-adrenal axis activity.

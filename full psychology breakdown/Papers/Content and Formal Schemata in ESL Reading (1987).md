# Content and Formal Schemata in ESL Reading

**Authors:** Patricia L. Carrell
**Year:** 1987
**Citations:** 403
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2307/3586498

## Abstract

This article reports the results of an experiment investigating the simultaneous effects on ESL reading comprehension of both culture-specific content schemata and formal schemata, as well as any potential interaction between them. In the study, high-intermediate ESL students read, recalled, and answered questions about each of two texts. For each of two groups of readers (students of Muslim and Catholic/Spanish backgrounds), one text had culturally familiar content, the other culturally unfamiliar content.

# The Associations of Clinicians’ Implicit Attitudes About Race With Medical Visit Communication and Patient Ratings of Interpersonal Care

**Authors:** Lisa A. Cooper, Thomas S. Inui
**Year:** 2012
**Citations:** 800
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2105/ajph.2011.300558

## Abstract

Clinician implicit race bias and race and compliance stereotyping are associated with markers of poor visit communication and poor ratings of care, particularly among Black patients.

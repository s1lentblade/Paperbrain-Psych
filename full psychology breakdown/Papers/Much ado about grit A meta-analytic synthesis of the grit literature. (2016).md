# Much ado about grit: A meta-analytic synthesis of the grit literature.

**Year:** 2016
**Citations:** 1,554

## Abstract

Grit has been presented as a higher order personality trait that is highly predictive of both success and performance and distinct from other traits such as conscientiousness. This paper provides a meta-analytic review of the grit literature with a particular focus on the structure of grit and the relation between grit and performance, retention, conscientiousness, cognitive ability, and demographic variables.

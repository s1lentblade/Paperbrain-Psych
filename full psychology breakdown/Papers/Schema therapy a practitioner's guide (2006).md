# Schema therapy a practitioner's guide

**Authors:** Jeffrey E. Young, Marjorie E. Weishaar
**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 1,548

## Abstract

Schema therapy is designed to meet the formidable challenges of treating personality disorders and other complex difficulties. This volume describes innovative ways to rapidly conceptualise challenging cases, explore the client's childhood, identify and modify self-defeating patterns and maximise the power of the therapeutic relationship

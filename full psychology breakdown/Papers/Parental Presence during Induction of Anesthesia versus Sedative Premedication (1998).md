# Parental Presence during Induction of Anesthesia versus Sedative Premedication

**Authors:** Zeev N. Kain, Maura B. Hofstadter
**Year:** 1998
**Citations:** 427
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1097/00000542-199811000-00015

## Abstract

Under the conditions of this study, oral midazolam is more effective than either parental presence or no intervention for managing a child's and parent's anxiety during the preoperative period.

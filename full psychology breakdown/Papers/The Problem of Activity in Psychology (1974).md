**Authors:** А. Н. Леонтьев
**Year:** 1974
**Citations:** 655
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2753/rpo1061-040513024

## Abstract

In recent years Soviet psychology has undergone rapid development in its various branches of applied research, while theoretical problems of general psychology have received much less attention. Nevertheless, Soviet psychological science, based on Marxist-Leninist philosophy, has proposed a fundamentally new approach to the mind and has introduced important Marxist categories that require further elaboration into psychology.

# Five New World Primates: A Study in Comparative Ecology

**Authors:** John Terborgh
**Year:** 1984
**Citations:** 1,256

## Abstract

Launching a new series, Monographs in Behavior and Ecology, this work is an intensive study of five species of New World monkeys--all omnivores with a diet of fruit and small prey.

# Altered body image: appearance‐related concerns of people with visible disfigurement

**Authors:** Nichola Rumsey, Wendy Garlick
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 282
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1365-2648.2004.03227.x

## Abstract

A significant proportion of participants experienced psychosocial distress in relation to their visible difference. Psychosocial needs were poorly met in current outpatient care provision, and a range of options could be considered to address these more effectively.

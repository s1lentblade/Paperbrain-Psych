**Authors:** Anthony D. Okely, John Patterson
**Year:** 2001
**Citations:** 476
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1097/00005768-200111000-00015

## Abstract

Fundamental movement skills are significantly associated with adolescents' participation in organized physical activity, but predict only a small portion of it.

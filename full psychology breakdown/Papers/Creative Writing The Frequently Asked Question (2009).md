## Abstract

Abstract With Creative Writing there is only one Frequently Asked Question and it is a loaded question, in Latin a num question, in Street a wy no question, a question that persists in defiance of the reality of arts education – can you teach writing? In universities in Britain, the subject is stigmatised as the curriculum innovation du jour, the Media Studies of the Noughties, too sexy for its student numbers.

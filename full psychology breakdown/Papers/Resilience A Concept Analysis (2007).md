# Resilience: A Concept Analysis

**Authors:** Marie Earvolino-Ramirez
**Year:** 2007
**Citations:** 655
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1744-6198.2007.00070.x

## Abstract

Resilience is being increasingly studied in innovative ways among diverse populations. The broadly accepted definition of the term, however, has evolved through literature related specifically to children and adolescents. This concept analysis can provide a fundamental definition of resilience that is derived from the evolution of the term but broadly supports contemporary applications. Resilience researchers can apply this definition when exploring the possibility of resilience-based interventions.

# Effects of Seclusion and Restraint in Adult Psychiatry: A Systematic Review

**Authors:** Marie Chieze, Othman Sentissi
**Year:** 2019
**Citations:** 310
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.3389/fpsyt.2019.00491

## Abstract

<b>Background:</b> Determining the clinical effects of coercion is a difficult challenge, raising ethical, legal, and methodological questions. Despite limited scientific evidence on effectiveness, coercive measures are frequently used, especially in psychiatry. This systematic review aims to search for effects of seclusion and restraint on psychiatric inpatients with wider inclusion of outcomes and study designs than former reviews. <b>Methods:</b> A systematic search was conducted following PRISMA guidelines, primarily through Pubmed, Embase, and CENTRAL.

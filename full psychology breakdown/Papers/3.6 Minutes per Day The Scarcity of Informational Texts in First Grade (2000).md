## Abstract

ABSTRACTS Although scholars have called for greater attention to informational texts in the early grades for some time, there have been few data available about the degree to which informational texts are actually included in early grade classrooms, and in what ways. This study provides basic, descriptive information about informational text experiences offered to children in 20 first‐grade classrooms selected from very low‐ and very high‐SES school districts. Each classroom was visited for four full days over the course of a school year.

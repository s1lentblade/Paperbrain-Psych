**Year:** 2005

## Abstract

Abstract This online resource is a good overview of the growth of psychotherapy integration into a mature, empirically supported, and international movement. It provides a state-of-the-art, comprehensive description of psychotherapy and its clinical practices by leading proponents. It covers topics such as cognitive-analytic therapy, integrative psychotherapy with culturally diverse clients, cognitive-behavioural analysis system, and blending spirituality with psychotherapy.

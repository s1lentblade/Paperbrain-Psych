# Change in children's competence beliefs and subjective task values across the elementary school years: A 3-year study.

**Authors:** Allan Wigfield, Phyllis C. Blumenfeld
**Year:** 1997
**Citations:** 1,005
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/0022-0663.89.3.451

## Abstract

The authors assessed change over 3 years in elementary school children's competence beliefs and subjective task value in the domains of math, reading, instrumental music, and sports. The longitudinal sample consisted of approximately 615 mostly White, lower middle to middle-class children. Stability correlations indicated moderate to strong stability in children's beliefs, especially older children's competence beliefs. The relation of children's ratings of their competence in each domain to estimates of their competence in those domains provided by both parents and teachers increased over the early elementary grades.

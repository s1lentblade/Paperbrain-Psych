## Abstract

There are several factors that influence the level of professional achievement. First and foremost, extensive experience of activi-ties in a domain is necessary to reach very high levels of performance. Extensive expe-rience in a domain does not, however, invari-ably lead to expert levels of achievement. When individuals are first introduced to a professional domain after completing their basic training and formal education, they often work as apprentices and are supervised by more-experienced professionals as they accomplish their work-related responsibili-ties.

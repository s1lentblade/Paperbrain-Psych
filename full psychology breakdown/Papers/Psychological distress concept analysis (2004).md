# Psychological distress: concept analysis

**Authors:** Sheila H. Ridner
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 792
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1046/j.1365-2648.2003.02938.x

## Abstract

Psychological distress is a serious problem faced by many of the people whom nurses encounter on a daily basis. An understanding of the concept of psychological distress will help nurses ameliorate this problem in patients. Nursing research related to the exploration of psychological distress is also needed.

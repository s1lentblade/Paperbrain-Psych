# Bullies and victims: Their ego picture, ideal ego picture and normative ego picture

**Authors:** Kaj Björkqvist, Kirsti Lagerspetz
**Year:** 1982
**Citations:** 220
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1467-9450.1982.tb00445.x

## Abstract

The ego picture, ideal ego picture and normative ego picture of bullies and their victims in a school situation were measured with semantic differentials. The bullies considered themselves to be dominant, had high ideals concerning dominance and thought this was what the social norms require. They felt themselves to be impulsive and lacking in self‐control. The victims considered themselves to be depressed, lacking in intelligence and personal attractiveness, and displayed in general feelings of inferiority.

# Doing the things we do: A grounded theory of academic procrastination.

**Authors:** Gregory Schraw, Lori Olafson
**Year:** 2007
**Citations:** 581
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/0022-0663.99.1.12

## Abstract

The authors conducted a grounded theory study of academic procrastination to explore adaptive and maladaptive aspects of procrastination and to help guide future empirical research. They discuss previous research on the definition and dimensionality of procrastination and describe the study in which interview data were collected in 4 stages, identifying 33 initial categories and 29 macrothemes. Findings were validated by member checks.

# Does Childhood Motor Skill Proficiency Predict Adolescent Fitness?

**Authors:** Lisa M. Barnett, John Beard
**Year:** 2008
**Citations:** 362
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1249/mss.0b013e31818160d3

## Abstract

Children with good object control skills are more likely to become fit adolescents. Fundamental motor skill development in childhood may be an important component of interventions aiming to promote long-term fitness.

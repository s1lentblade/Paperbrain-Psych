# Suicidal Ideation and Suicide Attempts in Body Dysmorphic Disorder

**Authors:** Katharine A. Phillips, Risa B. Weisberg
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 281
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.4088/jcp.v66n0607

## Abstract

Individuals with BDD have high rates of suicidal ideation and suicide attempts. Lifetime impairment due to BDD and certain comorbid disorders are associated with suicidality.

# The collected papers of David Rapaport

**Authors:** David Rapaport, Merton M. Gill
**Year:** 1967
**Citations:** 400

## Abstract

These collected papers re-introduce David Rapaport's work, the central objective of which was to develop a general psychological theory that encompasses both internal and external reality. The aim is to reappraise his position as one of the founders of contemporary psychoanalysis.

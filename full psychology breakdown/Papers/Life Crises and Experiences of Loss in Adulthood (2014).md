# Life Crises and Experiences of Loss in Adulthood

**Year:** 2014
**Citations:** 400
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.4324/9781315807171

## Abstract

This volume is a result of a Conference at the University of Trier, Germany, in July

1989. The goals of the Conference were (a) to provide an overview of recent

advances in research on critical lifc events and losses associated with them, (b) to

stimulate new approaches to the analysis of these events, and (c) to compare the

psychology of victims' experiences of stress and losses with the psychology of

observers' reactions to those victims. Papers presented were thoroughly discussed

by a panel that represented a good deal of expertise in this field of research. The

volume is organized into six interrelated parts. It ends with some methodological

comments.

# Adverse pregnancy outcomes: differences between US- and foreign-born women in major US racial and ethnic groups.

**Authors:** Gopal K. Singh, Stella M. Yu
**Year:** 1996
**Citations:** 385
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2105/ajph.86.6.837

## Abstract

Maternal nativity status, along with ethnicity, may serve as an important axis of differentiation in birth outcome studies. Further research needs to be conducted to assess the effects of behavioral, cultural, and psychosocial factors in explaining the nativity differentials observed here.

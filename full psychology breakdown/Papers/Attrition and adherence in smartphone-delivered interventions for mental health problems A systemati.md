# Attrition and adherence in smartphone-delivered interventions for mental health problems: A systematic and meta-analytic review.

**Authors:** Jake Linardon, Matthew Fuller‐Tyszkiewicz
**Year:** 2019
**Citations:** 607
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/ccp0000459

## Abstract

Study attrition and low adherence are common, problematic, and may undermine the validity of findings in RCTs of smartphone-delivered interventions for mental health problems. (PsycINFO Database Record (c) 2019 APA, all rights reserved).

# Mental health and well-being during the COVID-19 pandemic: longitudinal analyses of adults in the UK COVID-19 Mental Health &amp; Wellbeing study

**Authors:** Rory C. O’Connor, Kathryn A. Robb
**Year:** 2020
**Citations:** 1,092
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1192/bjp.2020.212

## Abstract

The mental health and well-being of the UK adult population appears to have been affected in the initial phase of the COVID-19 pandemic. The increasing rates of suicidal thoughts across waves, especially among young adults, are concerning.

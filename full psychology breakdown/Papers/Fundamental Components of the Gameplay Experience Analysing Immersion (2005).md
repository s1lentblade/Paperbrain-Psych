# Fundamental Components of the Gameplay Experience: Analysing Immersion

**Authors:** Laura Ermi, Frans Mäyrä
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 774
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.26503/dl.v2005i1.119

## Abstract

This paper presents a gameplay experience model, assesses its potential as a tool for research and presents some directions for future work. The presented model was born from observations among game-playing children and their non-player parents, which directed us to have a closer look at the complex nature of gameplay experience. Our research led into a heuristic gameplay experience model that identifies some of the key components and processes that are relevant in the experience of gameplay, with a particular focus on immersion. The model includes three components: sensory, challenge-based and imaginative immersion (SCI-model). The classification was assessed with self-evaluation questionna

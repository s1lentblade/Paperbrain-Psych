# Introducing the RECOLA multimodal corpus of remote collaborative and affective interactions

**Authors:** Fabien Ringeval, Denis Lalanne
**Year:** 2013
**Citations:** 742
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1109/fg.2013.6553805

## Abstract

We present in this paper a new multimodal corpus of spontaneous collaborative and affective interactions in French: RECOLA, which is being made available to the research community. Participants were recorded in dyads during a video conference while completing a task requiring collaboration. Different multimodal data, i.e., audio, video, ECG and EDA, were recorded continuously and synchronously. In total, 46 participants took part in the test, for which the first 5 minutes of interaction were kept to ease annotation.

## Abstract

AS FAR as anyone knows, different organisms are often in psychological states of exactly the same type at one time or another, and a given organism is often in psychological states of exactly the same type at different times. Whenever either is the case, we shall say of the psychological states of the organism(s) in question that they are type identical. One thing that currently fashionable theories in the philosophy of mind often try to do is characterize the conditions for type identity of psychological states.

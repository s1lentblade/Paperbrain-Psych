# Child behaviour problems and partner mental health as correlates of stress in mothers and fathers of children with autism

**Authors:** Richard P. Hastings
**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 534
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1046/j.1365-2788.2003.00485.x

## Abstract

Although requiring replication, the results suggest that stress in mothers of children with disabilities may be affected by the psychological health of other family members, whereas fathers' stress is affected more by other factors. Methodological and conceptual issues, and the practical implications of these results are discussed.

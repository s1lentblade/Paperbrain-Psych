# Coming to America: Developing Social Support Systems for International Students

**Authors:** Richard L. Hayes, Heng‐Rue Lin
**Year:** 1994
**Citations:** 281
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/j.2161-1912.1994.tb00238.x

## Abstract

Increasing numbers of international students are coming to America to pursue their education. College counselors face new challenges to be more culturally sensitive to the unique experience of these students in developing satisfying social relationships.

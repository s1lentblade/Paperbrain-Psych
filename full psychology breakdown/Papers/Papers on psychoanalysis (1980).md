## Abstract

This volume brings together many of the important writings of Hans Loewald, one of the major theoreticians of psychoanalysis today. Among other subjects, Dr. discusses the nature of the internalization processes and structure building, the nature and role of reality, pre oedipal modes of perceiving and the permanence of a psychotic core in every personality, the relationship of psychoanalysis to culture, mastery and defense, and the nature of time. [Loewald's] writing is stimulating and challenging, and his work contains some profound psychoanalytic insights.-Steven J.

# Pressure-pain threshold in human temporal region. Evaluation of a new pressure algometer

**Authors:** Kai Jensen, Ulf Lindblom
**Year:** 1986
**Citations:** 427
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/0304-3959(86)90235-6

## Abstract

A hand-held pressure algometer with a pressure sensitive strain gauge at the tip was used to measure the pressure-pain threshold (PPT) in the temporal region of healthy volunteers. Various sizes of circular tips and various application rates were tested before selecting an area of 0.5 cm2 and a constant application rate of 0.68 N X sec-1 for future use. A highly significant correlation was found between PPT values obtained from the two sides (of the head) (P less than 0.001) and between PPT values obtained with a 3-week interval (P less than 0.001).

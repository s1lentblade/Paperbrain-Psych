# Prevalence, Correlates, and Disability of Personality Disorders in the United States

**Authors:** Bridget F. Grant, Roger P. Pickering
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 726
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.4088/jcp.v65n0711

## Abstract

Personality disorders are prevalent in the general population and are generally highly associated with disability. This study highlights the need to develop more effective and targeted prevention and intervention initiatives for personality disorders.

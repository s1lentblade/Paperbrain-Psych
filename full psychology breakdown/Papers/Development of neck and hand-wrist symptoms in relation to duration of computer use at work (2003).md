# Development of neck and hand-wrist symptoms in relation to duration of computer use at work

**Authors:** Chris Jensen
**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 234
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.5271/sjweh.722

## Abstract

Limiting computer use to less than three-fourths of the worktime would help to prevent hand-wrist symptoms. Furthermore, low influence at work predicts both neck and hand-wrist symptoms.

# Psychosocial Risk Factors of Child and Adolescent Completed Suicide

**Authors:** Madelyn S. Gould
**Year:** 1996
**Citations:** 622
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/archpsyc.1996.01830120095016

## Abstract

Socioenvironmental circumstances add significantly to a teenager's risk of suicide. The overall effect size on increasing suicide risk of the psychosocial factors is comparable with that for diagnostic factors, highlighting the importance of considering socioenvironmental factors when assessing suicide risk.

# Evaluation of Training

**Authors:** Martha Reeves
**Year:** 1993
**Citations:** 914

## Abstract

This book is a practical guide for those involved in training or personnel management. It offers a simple explanation of the most common evaluation designs and methods and provides many examples of how evaluation has been put into practice in many organisations. It also gives practical advice about calculating the costs and benefits of training and about how to structure an evaluation report.

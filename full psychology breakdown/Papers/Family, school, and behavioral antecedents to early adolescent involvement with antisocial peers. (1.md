# Family, school, and behavioral antecedents to early adolescent involvement with antisocial peers.

**Authors:** Thomas J. Dishion, Martie L. Skinner
**Year:** 1991
**Citations:** 1,011
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/0012-1649.27.1.172

## Abstract

This study focuses on the prediction of early adolescent involvement with antisocial peers from boys' experiences in school, family, and behavior at age 10. Two hundred and six boys and their families were assessed at school, interviewed, observed in the home, and then followed up at age 12.

# Intensive Remedial Instruction for Children with Severe Reading Disabilities

**Authors:** Joseph K. Torgesen, Tim Conway
**Year:** 2001
**Citations:** 971
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1177/002221940103400104

## Abstract

Sixty children with severe reading disabilities were randomly assigned to two instructional programs that incorporated principles of effective instruction but differed in depth and extent of instruction in phonemic awareness and phonemic decoding skills. All children received 67.5 hours of one-to-one instruction in two 50-minute sessions per day for 8 weeks. Both instructional programs produced very large improvements in generalized reading skills that were stable over a 2-year follow-up period.

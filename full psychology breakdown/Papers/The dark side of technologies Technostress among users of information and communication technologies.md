# The dark side of technologies: Technostress among users of information and communication technologies

**Authors:** Marisa Salanova, Eva Cifré
**Year:** 2012
**Citations:** 541
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/00207594.2012.680460

## Abstract

This paper tests the structure and the predictors of two psychological experiences of technostress associated with the use of information and communication technologies (ICT), i.e., technostrain (users report feelings of anxiety, fatigue, scepticism and inefficacy beliefs related to the use of technologies) and technoaddiction (users feel bad due to an excessive and compulsive use of these technologies). The study included a sample of 1072 ICT users (N = 675 nonintensive ICT users and N = 397 intensive ICT users).

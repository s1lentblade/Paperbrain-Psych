**Authors:** James V. Wertsch
**Year:** 1984
**Citations:** 565
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/cd.23219842303

## Abstract

Vygotsky's ideas about the zone of proximal development are elucidated and extended by inclusion of the theoretical constructs of situation definition, intersubjectivity, and semiotic mediation.

# Towards a model of teacher well-being: personal and job resources involved in teacher burnout and engagement

**Authors:** Laura Bermejo Toro, Vicente Hernández
**Year:** 2015
**Citations:** 189
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/01443410.2015.1005006

## Abstract

Although much research has focused on the role of job demands and job resources in teacher well-being, few studies have targeted the function of personal variables.The aim of this study is to develop a comprehensive model of teacher well-being, using burnout and engagement in order to reflect, not only job demands and professional resources, but particularly the personal resources that characterise teacher well-being: self-efficacy, and cognitive and behavioural coping resources (proactive and reactive) related to three specific situational demands.

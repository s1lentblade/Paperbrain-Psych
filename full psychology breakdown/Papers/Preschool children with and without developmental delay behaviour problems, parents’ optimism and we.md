# Preschool children with and without developmental delay: behaviour problems, parents’ optimism and well‐being

**Authors:** Bruce L. Baker, Malin Olsson
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 353
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1365-2788.2005.00691.x

## Abstract

Interventions for parents that aim to enhance both parenting skills and psychological well-being should be available in preschool. It may be beneficial for such programmes to focus not only on behaviour management strategies aimed at child behaviour change, but also on parents' belief systems, with the aim of increasing dispositional optimism.

# Effects of changes in sitting work posture on static neck and shoulder muscle activity

**Authors:** K Schüldt, Ulf P. Arborelius
**Year:** 1986
**Citations:** 283
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/00140138608967266

## Abstract

Abstract In order to analyse the effect of changing the sitting posture on the level of neck and shoulder muscular activity, an electromyographic (EMG) study of ten healthy experienced female workers from an electronics plant was undertaken. A standardized, simulated task was performed in eight different sitting work postures. Using surface electrodes, the level of muscular activity was recorded as normalized, full-wave rectified low-pass filtered EMG.

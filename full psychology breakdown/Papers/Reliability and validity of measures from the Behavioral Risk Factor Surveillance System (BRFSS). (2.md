# Reliability and validity of measures from the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS).

**Authors:** David E. Nelson, Karin A. Mack
**Year:** 2001
**Citations:** 633

## Abstract

Most questions on the core BRFSS instrument were at least moderately reliable and valid, and many were highly reliable and valid. Additional research is needed for some measures.

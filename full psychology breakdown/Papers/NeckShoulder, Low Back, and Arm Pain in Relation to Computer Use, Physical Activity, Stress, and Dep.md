# Neck/Shoulder, Low Back, and Arm Pain in Relation to Computer Use, Physical Activity, Stress, and Depression Among Dutch Adolescents

**Authors:** A.C.M. Diepenmaat, Remy A. Hirasing
**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 304
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1542/peds.2004-2766

## Abstract

This study strengthens the findings that musculoskeletal pain is common among adolescents and is associated with depression and stress but not with computer use and physical activity.

# Achievement goals and achievement emotions: Testing a model of their joint relations with academic performance.

**Authors:** Reinhard Pekrun, Markus Maier
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 1,226

## Abstract

The authors propose a theoretical model linking achievement goals and achievement emotions to academic performance. This model was tested in a prospective study with undergraduates (N 213), using exam-specific assessments of both goals and emotions as predictors of exam performance in an introductory-level psychology course. The findings were consistent with the authors ’ hypotheses and supported all aspects of the proposed model.

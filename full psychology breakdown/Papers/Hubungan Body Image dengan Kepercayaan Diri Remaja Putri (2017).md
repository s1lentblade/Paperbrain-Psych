## Abstract

This study aims to describe the condition of teenage girl’s self-confidence and body image and to identify the relationship between teenage girl's body image with self confidence. This descriptive and correlation study using 77 teenage girls as sample. Sampling technique used stratified random sampling technique. The research used instrument Zila &amp; Ifdil Kepercayaan Diri Inventori and Zila &amp; Ifdil Body Images Inventory. Data were analyzed using descriptive analysis technique and correlational analysis.

**Authors:** Jon E. Grant, Marc N. Potenza
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 429
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/appi.ajp.162.11.2184

## Abstract

Impulse control disorders appear common among psychiatric inpatients. Additional, larger studies are needed to examine the prevalence of impulse control disorders in the general population and specific psychiatric groups.

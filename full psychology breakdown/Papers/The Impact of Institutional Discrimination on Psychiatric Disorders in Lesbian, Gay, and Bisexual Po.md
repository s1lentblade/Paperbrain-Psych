# The Impact of Institutional Discrimination on Psychiatric Disorders in Lesbian, Gay, and Bisexual Populations: A Prospective Study

**Authors:** Mark L. Hatzenbuehler, Deborah S. Hasin
**Year:** 2010
**Citations:** 879
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2105/ajph.2009.168815

## Abstract

Living in states with discriminatory policies may have pernicious consequences for the mental health of LGB populations. These findings lend scientific support to recent efforts to overturn these policies.

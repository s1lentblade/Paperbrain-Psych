# Youth-focused citizen science: Examining the role of environmental science learning and agency for conservation

**Authors:** Heidi L. Ballard, Emily Harris
**Year:** 2016
**Citations:** 358
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.biocon.2016.05.024

## Abstract

Citizen science by youth is rapidly expanding, but very little research has addressed the ways programs meet the dual goals of rigorous conservation science and environmental science education. We examined case studies of youth-focused community and citizen science (CCS) and analyzed the learning processes and outcomes, and stewardship activities for youth, as well as contributions to site and species management, each as conservation outcomes.

# Instagram, Flickr, or Twitter: Assessing the usability of social media data for visitor monitoring in protected areas

**Authors:** Henrikki Tenkanen, Tuuli Toivonen
**Year:** 2017
**Citations:** 425
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1038/s41598-017-18007-4

## Abstract

Social media data is increasingly used as a proxy for human activity in different environments, including protected areas, where collecting visitor information is often laborious and expensive, but important for management and marketing. Here, we compared data from Instagram, Twitter and Flickr, and assessed systematically how park popularity and temporal visitor counts derived from social media data perform against high-precision visitor statistics in 56 national parks in Finland and South Africa in 2014.

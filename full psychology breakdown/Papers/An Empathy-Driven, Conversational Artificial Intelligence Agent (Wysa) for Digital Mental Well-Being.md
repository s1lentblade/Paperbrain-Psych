# An Empathy-Driven, Conversational Artificial Intelligence Agent (Wysa) for Digital Mental Well-Being: Real-World Data Evaluation Mixed-Methods Study

**Authors:** Becky Inkster, Vinod Subramanian
**Year:** 2018
**Citations:** 911
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2196/12106

## Abstract

The real-world data evaluation findings on the effectiveness and engagement levels of Wysa app on users with self-reported symptoms of depression show promise. However, further work is required to validate these initial findings in much larger samples and across longer periods.

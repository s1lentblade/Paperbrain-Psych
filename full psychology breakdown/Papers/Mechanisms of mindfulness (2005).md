# Mechanisms of mindfulness

**Authors:** Shauna L. Shapiro, Ben Freedman
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 3,845
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/jclp.20237

## Abstract

Recently, the psychological construct mindfulness has received a great deal of attention. The majority of research has focused on clinical studies to evaluate the efficacy of mindfulness-based interventions. This line of research has led to promising data suggesting mindfulness-based interventions are effective for treatment of both psychological and physical symptoms. However, an equally important direction for future research is to investigate questions concerning mechanisms of action underlying mindfulness-based interventions. This theoretical paper proposes a model of mindfulness, in an effort to elucidate potential mechanisms to explain how mindfulness affects positive change. Potential

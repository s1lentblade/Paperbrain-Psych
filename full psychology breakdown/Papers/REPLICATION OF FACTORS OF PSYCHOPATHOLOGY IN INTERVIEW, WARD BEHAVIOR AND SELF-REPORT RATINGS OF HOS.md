## Abstract

In a prior study, we identified factors of psychopathology in the interview, ward behavior and self-report ratings of 124 depressed patients from 9 hospitals. The present study attempts to replicate these findings in a sample of 648 depressed patients from 10 hospitals. The criteria for patient selection and the factor analytic methods employed were identical in both studies. The major factors of psychopathology identified in the first study were replicated in the second study. Further, the loadings of the key items on these factors were highly similar in both studies.

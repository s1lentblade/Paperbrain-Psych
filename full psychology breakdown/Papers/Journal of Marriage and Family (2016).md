## Abstract

Abstract The Journal of Marriage and Family , which is affiliated with the National Council on Family Relations, has been the leading peer‐reviewed family research journal for over 70 years. It is an interdisciplinary journal that includes contributions from sociology, psychology, demography, economics, anthropology, history and human development and family sciences. This entry discusses its history and scope.

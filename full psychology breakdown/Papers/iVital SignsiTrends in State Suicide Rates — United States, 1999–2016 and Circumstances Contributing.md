# <i>Vital Signs:</i>Trends in State Suicide Rates — United States, 1999–2016 and Circumstances Contributing to Suicide — 27 States, 2015

**Authors:** Deborah M. Stone, Alex E. Crosby
**Year:** 2018
**Citations:** 625
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.15585/mmwr.mm6722a1

## Abstract

States can use a comprehensive evidence-based public health approach to prevent suicide risk before it occurs, identify and support persons at risk, prevent reattempts, and help friends and family members in the aftermath of a suicide.

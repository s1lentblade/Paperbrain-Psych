## Abstract

<b>Background</b>: As COVID-19 occurs suddenly and is highly contagious, this will inevitably cause people anxiety, depression, etc. The study on the public psychological states and its related factors during the COVID-19 outbreak is of practical significance. <b>Methods</b>: 600 valid questionnaires were received.

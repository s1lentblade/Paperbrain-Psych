# On Becoming Aware: A pragmatics of experiencing

**Authors:** Nathalie Depraz, Pierre Vermersch
**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 316

## Abstract

"This book searches for the sources and means for a disciplined practical approach to exploring human experience. The spirit of this book is pragmatic and relies on a Husserlian phenomenology primarily understood as a method of exploring our experience. The authors do not aim at a neo-Kantian a priori 'new theory' of experience but instead they describe a concrete activity: how we examine what we live through, how we become aware of our own mental life."--BOOK JACKET.

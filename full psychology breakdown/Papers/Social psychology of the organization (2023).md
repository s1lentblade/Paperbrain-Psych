## Abstract

This article reveals the relevance of the issue of the social organization formation. The difficulties that occur in defining the concept, structure, and classification and ensuring the diagnosis and development of various types of social organization are analyzed. The reliability of the organizational (corporate) security system is associated with overcoming these difficulties. Therefore, it is no coincidence that the most important task is to create a general theory of various types of social organization.

**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 257

## Abstract

Abstract From tiny to gigantic, from drab to remarkably beautiful, from harmless to venomous, lizards are spectacular products of natural selection. This book provides a comprehensive reference on lizards around the world. It covers species evolution, diversity, ecology, and biology. The authors of this book have studied and photographed members of almost all lizard families worldwide, and they bring to the book a deep knowledge based on extensive first-hand experience with the animals in their natural habitats.

# The meaning of music in the lives of older people: a qualitative study

**Authors:** Terrence Hays, Victor Minichiello
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 342
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1177/0305735605056160

## Abstract

This qualitative study describes the experience of music and focuses on the emotional, social, intellectual and spiritual well-being roles that music plays in the lives of older people. In-depth interviews were used to explore the meaning, importance and function of music for 52 older Australians living in the community aged 60 years and older. The findings revealed that music provides people with ways of understanding and developing their self-identity; connecting with others; maintaining well-being; and experiencing and expressing spirituality.

# Utfordringer med å veilede sykepleierstudenter i praksisstudier

**Authors:** Eva Aigeltinger, Venke Sørlie
**Year:** 2012
**Citations:** 36
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.4220/sykepleienf.2012.0084

## Abstract

Background: In Norwegian bachelor nurse education, 50% of the program is studies in the praxis arena where nurse clinicians have the day-to-day responsibility for student mentoring. Studies show that further knowledge and research are needed addressing clinical nurse instructors' experiences as student mentors.

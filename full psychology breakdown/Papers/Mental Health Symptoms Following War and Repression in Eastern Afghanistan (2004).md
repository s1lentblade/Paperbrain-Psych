# Mental Health Symptoms Following War and Repression in Eastern Afghanistan

**Authors:** Willem F. Scholte
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 386
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/jama.292.5.585

## Abstract

In this survey of inhabitants of Nangarhar province, Afghanistan, prevalence rates of having experienced multiple traumatic events and having symptoms of anxiety, depression, and PTSD were high. These findings suggest that mental health symptoms in this region should be addressed at the population and primary health care level.

# Prevalence of mental disorders among children exposed to war: a systematic review of 7,920 children

**Authors:** Vindya Attanayake, Edward J. Mills
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 346
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/13623690802568913

## Abstract

Worldwide, millions of children are affected by armed conflict. However, data on the prevalence of mental disorders among these children is sparse. We aimed to determine the prevalence of mental disorders among children affected by war using a systematic review and meta-regression analysis. We systematically reviewed existing literature to identify studies on prevalence of post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), anxiety, depression and psychosis among children exposed to armed conflict. We searched electronic databases and references listed in studies to obtain eligible studies.

# Assessing Resilience Across Cultures Using Mixed Methods: Construction of the Child and Youth Resilience Measure

**Authors:** Michael Ungar, Linda Liebenberg
**Year:** 2011
**Citations:** 705
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1177/1558689811400607

## Abstract

An international team of investigators in 11 countries have worked collaboratively to develop a culturally and contextually relevant measure of youth resilience, the Child and Youth Resilience Measure (CYRM-28). The team used a mixed methods design that facilitated understanding of both common and unique aspects of resilience across cultures. Quantitative and qualitative stages to its development ensure the CYRM-28 has good content-related validity across research sites.

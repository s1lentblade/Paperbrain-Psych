# Cultural memory studies : an international and interdisciplinary handbook

**Authors:** Astrid Erll, Sara B. Young
**Year:** 2008
**Citations:** 543
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1515/9783110207262

## Abstract

This handbook represents the interdisciplinary and international field of memory studies for the first time in one volume. Articles by renowned international scholars offer readers a unique overview of the key concepts of cultural memory studies. The handbook not only documents current research in an unprecedented way; it also serves as a forum for bringing together approaches from areas as varied as sociology, political sciences, history, theology, literary studies, media studies, philosophy, psychology, and neurosciences.

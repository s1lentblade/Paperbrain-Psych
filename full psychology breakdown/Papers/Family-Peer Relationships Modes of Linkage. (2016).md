# Family-Peer Relationships: Modes of Linkage.

**Authors:** Ross D. Parke, Gary W. Ladd
**Year:** 2016
**Citations:** 875

## Abstract

This volume provides an up-to-date overview of recent research concerning the links between family and peer systems. Considerable work in the past has focused on family issues or peer relationships, but these systems have typically been considered separately. This volume bridges the gap across these two important socialization contexts and provides insights into the processes that account for the links across the systems -- the ways in which the relationships between these systems shift across development.

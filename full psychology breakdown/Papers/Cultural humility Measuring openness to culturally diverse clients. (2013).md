# Cultural humility: Measuring openness to culturally diverse clients.

**Authors:** Joshua N. Hook, Shawn O. Utsey
**Year:** 2013
**Citations:** 1,019
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/a0032595

## Abstract

Building on recent theory stressing multicultural orientation, as well as the development of virtues and dispositions associated with multicultural values, we introduce the construct of cultural humility, defined as having an interpersonal stance that is other-oriented rather than self-focused, characterized by respect and lack of superiority toward an individual's cultural background and experience.

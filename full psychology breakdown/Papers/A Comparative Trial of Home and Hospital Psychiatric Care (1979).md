# A Comparative Trial of Home and Hospital Psychiatric Care

**Authors:** Fred R. Fenton
**Year:** 1979
**Citations:** 155
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/archpsyc.1979.01780100043003

## Abstract

The effectiveness of community-based treatment stressing home care was compared with hospital-based psychiatric care. One hundred and fifty-five patients destined for inpatient psychiatric care were randomly assigned to Home Care (76 patients) and to Hospital Care (79 patients). Symptoms, role functioning, and psychosocial burden on the family were similar at admission, one month, three months, six months, and one year. The mean in-hospital stay of Hospital Care patients was 41.7 days compared with a mean stay of 14.5 days for Home Care patients.

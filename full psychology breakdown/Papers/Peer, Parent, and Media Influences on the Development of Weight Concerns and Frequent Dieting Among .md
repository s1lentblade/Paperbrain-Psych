# Peer, Parent, and Media Influences on the Development of Weight Concerns and Frequent Dieting Among Preadolescent and Adolescent Girls and Boys

**Authors:** Alison E. Field, Graham A. Colditz
**Year:** 2001
**Citations:** 618
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1542/peds.107.1.54

## Abstract

Our results suggest that parents and the media influence the development of weight concerns and weight control practices among preadolescents and adolescents. However, there are gender differences in the relative importance of these influences.

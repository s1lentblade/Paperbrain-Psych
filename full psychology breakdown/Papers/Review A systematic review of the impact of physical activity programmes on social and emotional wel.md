# Review: A systematic review of the impact of physical activity programmes on social and emotional well‐being in at‐risk youth

**Authors:** David R. Lubans, Nicole J. Lubans
**Year:** 2011
**Citations:** 226
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1475-3588.2011.00623.x

## Abstract

Due to the mixed findings and the high risk of bias, it is difficult to determine the efficacy of physical activity programmes for improving social and emotional well being in at-risk youth.

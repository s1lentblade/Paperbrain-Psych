# Getting even or moving on? Power, procedural justice, and types of offense as predictors of revenge, forgiveness, reconciliation, and avoidance in organizations.

**Authors:** Karl Aquino, Robert J. Bies
**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 705
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/0021-9010.91.3.653

## Abstract

A field study and an experimental study examined relationships among organizational variables and various responses of victims to perceived wrongdoing. Both studies showed that procedural justice climate moderates the effect of organizational variables on the victim's revenge, forgiveness, reconciliation, or avoidance behaviors.

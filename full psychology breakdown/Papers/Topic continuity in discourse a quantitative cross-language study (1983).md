## Abstract

The functional notion of &amp;#8220;topic&amp;#8221; or &amp;#8220;topicality&amp;#8221; has suffered, traditionally, from two distinct drawbacks. First, it has remained largely ill defined or intuitively defined. And second, quite often its definition boiled down to structure-dependent circularity. This volume represents a major departure from past practices, without rejecting both their intuitive appeal and the many good results yielded by them.

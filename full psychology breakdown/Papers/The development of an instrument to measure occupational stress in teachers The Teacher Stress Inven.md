## Abstract

The content validity, factorial validity, and internal consistency reliability of a scale developed to assess occupational stress in teachers were investigated. The content validity of the scale was determined from expert opinion and appraisal data collected from 92 teachers, stress researchers and practitioners. Data collected from two samples of special education teachers ( n = 370; n = 371) and one sample of regular education teachers ( n = 433) were then subjected to factor analyses followed by varimax and oblique rotations.

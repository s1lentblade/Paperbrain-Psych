**Authors:** Harry Collins, Robert Evans
**Year:** 2013
**Citations:** 55

## Abstract

This article describes a new research method called the Imitation Game. The method is based on the idea of ‘interactional expertise’, which distinguishes discursive performance from practical expertise and can be used to investigate the relationship between groups that diverge culturally or experientially. We explain the theory that underpins the method and report results from a number of empirical trials.

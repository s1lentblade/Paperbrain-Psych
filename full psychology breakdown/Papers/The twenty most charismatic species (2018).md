# The twenty most charismatic species

**Authors:** Céline Albert, Franck Courchamp
**Year:** 2018
**Citations:** 376

## Abstract

Charisma is a term commonly used in conservation biology to describe species. However, as the term "charismatic species" has never been properly defined, it needs to be better characterized to fully meet its potential in conservation biology. To provide a more complete depiction, we collected information from four different sources to define the species currently considered to be the most charismatic and to understand what they represent to the Western public.

# Clases de literatura : Berkeley, 1980

**Authors:** Julio Cortázar, Carles Garriga
**Year:** 2017
**Citations:** 20

## Abstract

This book contains unedited transcripts of thirteen hours of Julio Cortázar's recorded lessons at Berkeley in 1980. The topics cover a wide variety of subjects, aspects of the Fantastic tale, musicality, humor, eroticism, and playfulness in literature, imagination and realism, social literature and language traps

## Abstract

Purpose The purpose of this paper is to show that development of competencies needed to be effective managers and leaders requires program design and teaching methods focused on learning. This is the introductory essay to this special issue of JMD . Design/methodology/approach Competencies are defined and an overview is provided for the eight papers that will follow with original research on competencies, their link to performance in various occupations, and their development. Findings Emotional, social and cognitive intelligence competencies predict effectiveness in professional, management and leadership roles in many sectors of society.

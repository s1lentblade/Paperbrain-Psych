## Abstract

<b>Background:</b> Saudi Arabia has taken unprecedented and stringent preventive and precautionary measures against COVID-19 to control its spread, safeguard citizens and ensure their well-being. Public adherence to preventive measures is influenced by their knowledge and attitude toward COVID-19. This study investigated the knowledge, attitudes, and practices of the Saudi public, toward COVID-19, during the pandemic. <b>Methods:</b> This is a cross-sectional study, using data collected via an online self-reported questionnaire, from 3,388 participants.

# An fMRI Stroop Task Study of Ventromedial Prefrontal Cortical Function in Pathological Gamblers

**Authors:** Marc N. Potenza, John C. Gore
**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 400
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/appi.ajp.160.11.1990

## Abstract

Pathological gamblers share many neural correlates of Stroop task performance with healthy subjects but differ in a brain region previously implicated in disorders characterized by poor impulse control.

# The nature of cognitive styles: Problems and promise in educational practice

**Authors:** Samuel Messick
**Year:** 1984
**Citations:** 623
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/00461528409529283

## Abstract

This article examines characteristic features of cognitive styles and the various ways in which styles differ from one another and from intellective abilities. These distinctions are integrated into a unified framework that serves to define cognitive styles in contrast not only to abilities but to other types of stylistic variables.

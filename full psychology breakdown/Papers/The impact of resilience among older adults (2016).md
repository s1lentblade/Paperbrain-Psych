# The impact of resilience among older adults

**Authors:** Stephanie MacLeod, Ellen Wicker
**Year:** 2016
**Citations:** 632
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1016/j.gerinurse.2016.02.014

## Abstract

The purpose of this literature review was to provide an overview of resilience for the purpose of informing potential intervention designs that may benefit older adults. While numerous reviews have focused on various specific aspects of resilience, none have provided the necessary information required to design an effective resilience intervention. Research examining resilience suggests that older adults are capable of high resilience despite socioeconomic backgrounds, personal experiences, and declining health.

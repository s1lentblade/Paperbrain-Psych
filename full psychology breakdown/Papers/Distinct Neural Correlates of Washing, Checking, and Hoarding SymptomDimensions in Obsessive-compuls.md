# Distinct Neural Correlates of Washing, Checking, and Hoarding SymptomDimensions in Obsessive-compulsive Disorder

**Authors:** David Mataix‐Cols, Mary L. Phillips
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 934
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/archpsyc.61.6.564

## Abstract

The findings suggest that different obsessive-compulsive symptom dimensions are mediated by relatively distinct components of frontostriatothalamic circuits implicated in cognitive and emotion processing. Obsessive-compulsive disorder may be best conceptualized as a spectrum of multiple, potentially overlapping syndromes rather than a unitary nosologic entity.

# Factorial validity of the Maslach Burnout Inventory (MBI-HSS) among Spanish professionals

**Authors:** Pedro R. Gil‐Monte
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 324
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1590/s0034-89102005000100001

## Abstract

The results show that the MBI-HSS offers factorial validity and its scales present internal consistency to evaluate the quality of working life for Spanish professionals.

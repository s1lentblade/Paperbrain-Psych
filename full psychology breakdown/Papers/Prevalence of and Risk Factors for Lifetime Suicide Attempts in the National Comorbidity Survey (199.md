# Prevalence of and Risk Factors for Lifetime Suicide Attempts in the National Comorbidity Survey

**Authors:** Ronald C. Kessler, Ellen E. Walters
**Year:** 1999
**Citations:** 2,523
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/archpsyc.56.7.617

## Abstract

Prevention efforts should focus on planned attempts because of the rapid onset and unpredictability of unplanned attempts. More research is needed on the determinants of unplanned attempts.

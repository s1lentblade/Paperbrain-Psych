# Embodiment as a Paradigm for Anthropology

**Authors:** Thomas J. Csordas
**Year:** 2002
**Citations:** 205
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1007/978-1-137-08286-2_3

## Abstract

The purpose of this essay is not to argue that the human body is an important object of anthropological study, but that a paradigm of embodiment can be elaborated for the study of culture and the self.

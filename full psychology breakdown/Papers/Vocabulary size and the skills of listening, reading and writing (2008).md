# Vocabulary size and the skills of listening, reading and writing

**Authors:** Lars Stenius Stæhr
**Year:** 2008
**Citations:** 541
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/09571730802389975

## Abstract

This paper presents an empirical study investigating the relationship between vocabulary size and the skills of listening, reading and writing in English as a foreign language (EFL). The participants were 88 EFL learners from lower secondary education whose language skills were assessed as part of the national school leaving examination in Denmark. Learners' receptive vocabulary size was found to be strongly associated with their reading and writing abilities and moderately associated with their listening ability.

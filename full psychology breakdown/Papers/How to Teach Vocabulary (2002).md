# How to Teach Vocabulary

**Authors:** Scott Thornbury
**Year:** 2002
**Citations:** 562

## Abstract

There has been a revival ofinterest in vocabulary teaching in recent years. This is partly due to the recent availability of computerised databases of words (or corpora), and party due to the development of new approaches to language teaching which are much more word centred, such as the lexical approach.

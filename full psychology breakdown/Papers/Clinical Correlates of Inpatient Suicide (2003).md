# Clinical Correlates of Inpatient Suicide

**Authors:** Katie A. Busch, Douglas Jacobs
**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 740
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.4088/jcp.v64n0105

## Abstract

Standard risk assessments and standard precautions used were of limited value in protecting this group from suicide. Adding severity of anxiety and agitation to our current assessments may help identify patients at acute risk and suggest effective treatment interventions. The importance of a matched comparison group to ascertain if this sample can be blindly discriminated from inpatients who do not commit suicide is clear.

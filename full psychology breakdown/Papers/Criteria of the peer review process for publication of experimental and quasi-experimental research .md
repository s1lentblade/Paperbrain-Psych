# Criteria of the peer review process for publication of experimental and quasi-experimental research in Psychology: A guide for creating research papers

**Authors:** Manuel M. Ramos‐Álvarez, Andrés Catena
**Year:** 2008
**Citations:** 195

## Abstract

"Experimental research in Psychology is characterized by ensuring a method that guarantees objectivity, reliability, validity and replication of results. In this theoretical study we propose a set of criteria for the preparation and review of quasiexperimental and experimental research manuscripts, which follows such methodological objective dictates. These criteria are based on a review of structural aspects in experimental research, in the modern theory of psychological theorization, and in the validity theory of scientific research.

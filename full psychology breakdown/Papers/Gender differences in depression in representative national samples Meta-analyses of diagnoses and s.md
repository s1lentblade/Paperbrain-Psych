# Gender differences in depression in representative national samples: Meta-analyses of diagnoses and symptoms.

**Authors:** Rachel H. Salk, Lyn Y. Abramson
**Year:** 2017
**Citations:** 2,511

## Abstract

In 2 meta-analyses on gender differences in depression in nationally representative samples, we advance previous work by including studies of depression diagnoses and symptoms to (a) estimate the magnitude of the gender difference in depression across a wide array of nations and ages; (b) use a developmental perspective to elucidate patterns of gender differences across the life span; and (c) incorporate additional theory-driven moderators (e.g., gender equity).

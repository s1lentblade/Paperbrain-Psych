# Beyond Exchange: APPRAISAL Systems in English

**Authors:** Joe Martin
**Year:** 2000
**Citations:** 1,142
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1093/oso/9780198238546.003.0008

## Abstract

Abstract In this chapter Martin examines evaluative lexis expressing the speaker or writer’s opinion on, very broadly, the good/bad parameter. His study is set within the systemic functional linguistic tradition; and the account is therefore couched in terms of systems: sets of options which are available to the speaker or writer covering the meanings that can be and are typically expressed in particular contexts, and the linguistic means of expressing them.

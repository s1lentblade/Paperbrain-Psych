# Causal Models: How People Think About the World and Its Alternatives

**Authors:** Steven A. Sloman
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 463
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1093/acprof:oso/9780195183115.001.0001

## Abstract

Sloman offers a conceptual introduction to key mathematical ideas, focusing on the intuitions rather than the theorems. He tries to show why the ideas are important to understanding how people explain things, &amp; why it is central to human action to think not only about the world as it is, but also about the world as it could be.

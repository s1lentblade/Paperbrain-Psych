# Violence : six sideways reflections

**Authors:** Slavoj Žižek
**Year:** 2008
**Citations:** 1,304

## Abstract

With the help of Marx, Engels, Sartre, Hegel, Kierkegaard, Lacan, Brecht and many more, Žižek examines the hidden causes of violence, delving into the supposed 'divine violence' which propels suicide bombers and the unseen 'systemic' violence which lies behind outbursts, from Parisian suburbia to New Orleans.

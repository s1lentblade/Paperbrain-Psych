# Frames of reference for the assessment of learning disabilities : new views on measurement issues

**Authors:** G. Reid Lyon
**Year:** 1994
**Citations:** 598

## Abstract

This valuable reference offers an in-depth look at two fundamental concerns facing those who work with children with learning disabilities (LD)--identification and assessment. Experts describe and examine assessment tools and discuss how the data can be translated into effective instructional strategies.

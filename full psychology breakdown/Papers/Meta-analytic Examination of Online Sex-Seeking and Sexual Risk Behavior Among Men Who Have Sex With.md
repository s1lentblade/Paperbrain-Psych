# Meta-analytic Examination of Online Sex-Seeking and Sexual Risk Behavior Among Men Who Have Sex With Men

**Authors:** Adrian Liau, Gary Marks
**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 416
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1097/01.olq.0000204710.35332.c5

## Abstract

A substantial percentage of MSM use the Internet to look for sex partners, and those who do are more likely to engage in unprotected sex. Additional research is needed to determine whether the Internet may increase risk behavior beyond that which occurs when men meet partners at offline venues.

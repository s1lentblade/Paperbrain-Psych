# A system for image-text relations in new (and old) media

**Authors:** Radan Martinec
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 552
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1177/1470357205055928

## Abstract

This article presents a generalized system of image–text relations which applies to different genres of multimodal discourse in which images and texts co-occur. It combines two kinds of relations – the relative status of images and text, and how they relate to one another in terms of logico–semantics. Every instance of an image–text combination in the data sample is described by a selection of features from the system. The units of images and text between which the relations obtain are identified and the realizations of the logico–semantic and status relations are specified, both for the human analyst and a machine.

# Social gerontology: A multidisciplinary perspective

**Authors:** Nancy R. Hooyman, H. Asuman Kiyak
**Year:** 1988
**Citations:** 821

## Abstract

Appropriate for sociology, psychology, and nursing students, this textbook examines the biological and physiological changes that affect older people's daily functioning, their risk of chronic diseases, the psychological changes that can occur, and the social implications of aging. The accompanying guide suggests articles to read for research paper

# Does old age reduce the risk of anxiety and depression? A review of epidemiological studies across the adult life span

**Authors:** Anthony F. Jorm
**Year:** 2000
**Citations:** 651
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1017/s0033291799001452

## Abstract

There is some evidence that ageing is associated with an intrinsic reduction in susceptibility to anxiety and depression. However, longitudinal studies covering the adult life span are needed to distinguish ageing from cohort effects. More attention needs to be given to understanding the mechanism behind any ageing-related reduction in risk for anxiety and depression with age. Possible factors are decreased emotional responsiveness with age, increased emotional control and psychological immunization to stressful experiences.

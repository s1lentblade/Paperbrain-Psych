# Unpacking Authoritative Parenting: Reassessing a Multidimensional Construct

**Authors:** Marjory Roberts Gray, Laurence Steinberg
**Year:** 1999
**Citations:** 834
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2307/353561

## Abstract

This study examines the independent and joint contributions of three core dimensions of authoritative parenting-acceptance-involvement, strictnesssupervision, and psychological autonomy granting-to adolescent adjustment. A sample of 8, 700 14- to IS-year-olds completed questionnaires that included indices of authoritative parenting and a set of instruments assessing different aspects of adjustment. Behavior problems were related more strongly to behavioral control than to psychological autonomy granting.

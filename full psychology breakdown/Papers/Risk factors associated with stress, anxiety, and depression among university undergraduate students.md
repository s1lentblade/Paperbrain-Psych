# Risk factors associated with stress, anxiety, and depression among university undergraduate students

**Authors:** Mohammad Mofatteh
**Year:** 2020
**Citations:** 498
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.3934/publichealth.2021004

## Abstract

It is well-known that prevalence of stress, anxiety, and depression is high among university undergraduate students in developed and developing countries. Students entering university are from different socioeconomic background, which can bring a variety of mental health risk factors. The aim of this review was to investigate present literatures to identify risk factors associated with stress, anxiety, and depression among university undergraduate students in developed and developing countries.

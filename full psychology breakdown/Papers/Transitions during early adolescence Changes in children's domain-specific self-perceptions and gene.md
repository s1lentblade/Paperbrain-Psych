# Transitions during early adolescence: Changes in children's domain-specific self-perceptions and general self-esteem across the transition to junior high school.

**Authors:** Allan Wigfield, Carol Midgley
**Year:** 1991
**Citations:** 989
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/0012-1649.27.4.552

## Abstract

Young adolescents' (N=1,850) achievement self-perceptions in 4 activity domains (math, English, social acitivities, and sports) and self-esteem were examined across the transition from elementary to junior high school

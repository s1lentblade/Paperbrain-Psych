# Gender differences in unipolar depression: an update of epidemiological findings and possible explanations

**Authors:** Christine Kuehner
**Year:** 2003
**Citations:** 835
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1034/j.1600-0447.2003.00204.x

## Abstract

There is a need for more integrative models taking into account psychological, psychosocial, and macrosocial risk factors as well as their interactions, which also connect these factors with physiological and endocrine responses. Furthermore, it is conceivable that across the life span, as well as across cultural settings, individual risk factors will add with varying emphasis to the higher prevalence of depression in women.

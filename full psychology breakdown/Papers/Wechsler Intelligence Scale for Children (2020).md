# Wechsler Intelligence Scale for Children

**Authors:** National Cancer Institute
**Year:** 2020
**Citations:** 702
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.32388/1lsxek

## Abstract

An individually administered clinical instrument for assessing the cognitive ability of children aged 6 years through 16 years 11 months.It is a psychometric test that assesses a child's current cognitive abilities in both verbal and nonverbal areas, including verbal comprehension, perceptual reasoning, working memory, and processing speed.It was first published in 1949 and revised in 1974 (WISC-R).

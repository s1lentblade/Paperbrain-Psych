# A Rasch-based validation of the Vocabulary Size Test

**Authors:** David Beglar
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 366
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1177/0265532209340194

## Abstract

The primary purpose of this study was to provide preliminary validity evidence for a 140-item form of the Vocabulary Size Test, which is designed to measure written receptive knowledge of the first 14,000 words of English. Nineteen native speakers of English and 178 native speakers of Japanese participated in the study. Analyses based on the Rasch model were focused on several aspects of Messick’s validation framework.

# The Use of Song in Teaching English for Junior High School Student

**Authors:** Muhamad Sofian Hadi
**Year:** 2019
**Citations:** 56
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.24853/elif.1.2.107-112

## Abstract

The aim of this study is to investigate in detail the use of song in teaching English for Junior High School student. This study is action researches conducted at Dharma Karya UT Junior High School class VII with participant number are 25 students. Data obtained from the result of test in each cycle. The findings showed that the learning process using song makes the students become more active in their interaction, it is showed by their participation level during the teaching is increased. Another finding also revealed that there is a significant improvement in the student’s English language skills using song from preliminary to cycle II stage in the research process.

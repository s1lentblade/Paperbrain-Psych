# The coach‐coachee relationship in executive coaching: A field study

**Authors:** Louis Baron, Lucie Morin
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 266
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/hrdq.20009

## Abstract

Abstract Numerous authors have suggested that the working relationship between coach and coachee constitutes an essential condition to the success of executive coaching. This study empirically investigated the links between the coach‐coachee relationship and the success of a coaching intervention in an organizational setting. Data were collected from two samples: 73 managers who received executive coaching for a period of eight months and 24 coaches. Results from 31 coach‐coachee dyads were analyzed.

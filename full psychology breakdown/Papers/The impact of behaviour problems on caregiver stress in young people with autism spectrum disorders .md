# The impact of behaviour problems on caregiver stress in young people with autism spectrum disorders

**Authors:** Luc Lecavalier, James Wiltz
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 1,151
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1365-2788.2005.00732.x

## Abstract

Results of this study suggested that it is a specific group of externalized behaviours that are the most strongly associated with both parent and teacher stress. Results were discussed from methodological and conceptual perspectives.

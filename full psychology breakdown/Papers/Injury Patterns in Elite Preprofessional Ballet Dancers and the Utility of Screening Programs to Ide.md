# Injury Patterns in Elite Preprofessional Ballet Dancers and the Utility of Screening Programs to Identify Risk Characteristics

**Authors:** Jennifer M. Gamboa, Andrea Fergus
**Year:** 2008
**Citations:** 279
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2519/jospt.2008.2390

## Abstract

Distribution of injuries was similar to that of other studies. Injury rates were lower than most reported rates, except when expressed per 1000 hours of dance. Few differences were found between injured and noninjured dancers. These findings should be considered when designing and implementing screening programs.

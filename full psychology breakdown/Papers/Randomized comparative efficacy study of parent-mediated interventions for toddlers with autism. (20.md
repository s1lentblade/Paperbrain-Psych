# Randomized comparative efficacy study of parent-mediated interventions for toddlers with autism.

**Authors:** Connie Kasari, Kathleen Berry
**Year:** 2015
**Citations:** 393
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/a0039080

## Abstract

These data highlight the benefit of a brief, targeted, parent-mediated intervention on child outcomes. Future studies may consider the combination of JASPER and PEI treatments for optimal parent and child outcomes. Trial registry no. NCT00999778.

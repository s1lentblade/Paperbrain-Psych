# Risk Factors for Suicide Completion in Major Depression: A Case-Control Study of Impulsive and Aggressive Behaviors in Men

**Authors:** Alexandre Dumais, Gustavo Turecki
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 586
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/appi.ajp.162.11.2116

## Abstract

Impulsive-aggressive personality disorders and alcohol abuse/dependence were two independent predictors of suicide in major depression, and impulsive and aggressive behaviors seem to underlie these risk factors. A developmental hypothesis of suicidal behavior, with impulsive and aggressive behaviors as the starting point, is discussed.

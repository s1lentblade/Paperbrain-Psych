# The Chestnut Lodge Follow-up Study

**Authors:** Thomas H. McGlashan
**Year:** 1984
**Citations:** 444
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/archpsyc.1984.01790170047006

## Abstract

Chestnut Lodge is a small, private psychiatric hospital in Rockville, Md, specializing in the long-term residential treatment of severely ill (and usually chronic) psychotic and borderline patients. Four hundred forty-six (72%) of the patients treated at Chestnut Lodge between 1950 and 1975 were followed up an average of 15 years later. This first report details the rationale and methods for this investigation.

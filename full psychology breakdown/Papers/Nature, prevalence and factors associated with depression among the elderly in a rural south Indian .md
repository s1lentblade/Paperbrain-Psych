# Nature, prevalence and factors associated with depression among the elderly in a rural south Indian community

**Authors:** Anto P. Rajkumar, K. S. Jacob
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 184
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1017/s1041610209008527

## Abstract

Geriatric depression is prevalent in rural south India. Poverty and physical ill health are risk factors for depression among elderly while good social support is protective.

# The abused child: II. A typology for classification and treatment

**Authors:** MSW Serapio Richard Zalba
**Year:** 1967
**Citations:** 43
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1093/sw/12.1.70

## Abstract

A problem/treatment typology is presented for families in which there has been physical abuse (i.e., injury) of children by parents. Each of the categories specifies (1) the immediate danger to the child, (2) the locus of the dysfunction in the child-parent relationship, and (3) the immediate reason for the aggression/abuse. In addition, treatment objectives are outlined and treatment strategy is linked, point by point, with the objectives.

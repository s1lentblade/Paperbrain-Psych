## Abstract

A long journey for the families. The family in psychiatry over the 20th century Some of the major themes developed in the sociology of mental health and in psychiatry are presented here specifically from the point of view of how the family has been perceived over the 20th century. Historically, the family was initially considered the « cause » of mental health problems. Today, in the age of psychiatrie desinstitutionalization, it has become a « solution » for keeping patients in their natural living environment.

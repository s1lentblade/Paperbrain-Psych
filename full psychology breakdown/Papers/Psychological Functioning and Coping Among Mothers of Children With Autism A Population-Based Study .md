# Psychological Functioning and Coping Among Mothers of Children With Autism: A Population-Based Study

**Authors:** Guillermo Montes, Jill S. Halterman
**Year:** 2007
**Citations:** 380
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1542/peds.2006-2819

## Abstract

Mothers of children with autism showed remarkable strengths in the parent-child relationship, social support, and stability of the household in the context of high stress and poorer mental health.

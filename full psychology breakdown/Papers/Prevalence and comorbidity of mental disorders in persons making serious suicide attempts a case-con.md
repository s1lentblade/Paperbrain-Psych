# Prevalence and comorbidity of mental disorders in persons making serious suicide attempts: a case-control study

**Authors:** Annette L. Beautrais, Sharon Nightingale
**Year:** 1996
**Citations:** 663
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/ajp.153.8.1009

## Abstract

Individuals who made serious suicide attempts had high rates of mental disorders and of comorbid disorders. Subjects with high levels of psychiatric comorbidity had markedly high risks of serious suicide attempts.

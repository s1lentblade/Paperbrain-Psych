# Cardiorespiratory differentiation of musically-induced emotions.

**Authors:** Ivan Nyklíček, Lorenz J.P. van Doornen
**Year:** 1997
**Citations:** 311
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1176/appi.ps.57.3.366

## Abstract

Religion is an important issue for patients with schizophrenia, and it is often not related to the content of their delusions. Clinicians were commonly not aware of their patients' religious involvement, even if they reported feeling comfortable with such an issue.

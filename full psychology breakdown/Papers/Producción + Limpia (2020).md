# Producción + Limpia

**Year:** 2020
**Citations:** 45
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.22507/pml

## Abstract

In this work, the coagulant properties of chitosan were investigated, finding that it is a linear cationic polymer, biodegradable, with excellent characteristics as a coagulant, due to the presence of amino groups in its structure that confer the ability to reduce the presence of colloids in water.Thus, the objective of the present work was to prepare a natural coagulant from chitosan, originating from fish scales of the Mojarra Lora (Oreochromis niloticus), by the homogeneous chemical method.First, the chitin was separated from the scales, through depigmentation, deproteinization, demineralization and deacetylation processes (obtaining chitosan); Which had a yield in the process of 78%, 78.

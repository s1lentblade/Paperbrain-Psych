# Depression and anxiety in parents of children with intellectual and developmental disabilities: A systematic review and meta-analysis

**Authors:** Nathaniel Scherer, Hannah Kuper
**Year:** 2019
**Citations:** 365
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0219888

## Abstract

Results indicate elevated levels of depressive symptoms amongst parents of children with IDD. Quality concerns amongst the existing literature support the need for further research, especially in low- and middle-income countries.

# Islamic revivalism and Kurdish nationalism in Sheikh Ubeydullah’s poetic oeuvre

**Authors:** Kamal Soleimani
**Year:** 2015
**Citations:** 42
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.33182/ks.v4i1.404

## Abstract

This article investigates Sheikh Ubeydullah of Nehri’s Kurdish-Islamic revivalist project based on a close reading of his Mesnewi. This article primarily focuses on the fusion of the Sheikh’s Islamic revivalism with his Kurdish nationalism in his poetic work.

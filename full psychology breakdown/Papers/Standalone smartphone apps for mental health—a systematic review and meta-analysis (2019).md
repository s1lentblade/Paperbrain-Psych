# Standalone smartphone apps for mental health—a systematic review and meta-analysis

**Authors:** Kiona K. Weisel, David Daniel Ebert
**Year:** 2019
**Citations:** 475
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1038/s41746-019-0188-8

## Abstract

While smartphone usage is ubiquitous, and the app market for smartphone apps targeted at mental health is growing rapidly, the evidence of standalone apps for treating mental health symptoms is still unclear. This meta-analysis investigated the efficacy of standalone smartphone apps for mental health. A comprehensive literature search was conducted in February 2018 on randomized controlled trials investigating the effects of standalone apps for mental health in adults with heightened symptom severity, compared to a control group.

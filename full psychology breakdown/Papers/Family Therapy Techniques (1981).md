# Family Therapy Techniques

**Authors:** Salvador Minuchin, H. Charles Fishman
**Year:** 1981
**Citations:** 1,269
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.4159/9780674041110

## Abstract

Minuchin traces for the first time the minute operations of day-to-day practice. Having achieved renown for his theoretical breakthroughs and his success at treatment, he now explains those precise and difficult maneuvers that constitute his art. The book thus codifies the method of one of the country’s most successful practitioners.

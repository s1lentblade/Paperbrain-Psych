## Abstract

This new volume is a revision and enlargement of an original workbook which appeared in 1952 under the title<i>Diagnostic Manual in Speech Correction</i>. Although designed for speech clinicians, it actually serves better for students concerned with the compilation of case histories and with interviewing in its basic form. The supplementary discussions and outlines for dysphasia, cerebral palsy, cleft palate, laryngectomy, and stuttering merely outline pertinent facts in the taking of a case history instead of offering a thorough discussion of the particular subject.

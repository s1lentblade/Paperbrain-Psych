# Grief Counseling and Grief Therapy: A Handbook for the Mental Health Practitioner

**Authors:** J. William Worden
**Year:** 1982
**Citations:** 805

## Abstract

In this updated and revised, third edition of his classic text, Worden incorporates his thinking on bereavement from extensive research, clinical work and the best of contemporary literature. Readers will find some refinements and several new areas of discussion. The model for understanding mourning not only includes the four Tasks of Mourning but his seven Mediators of Mourning as well. Important new and expanded materials address special types of losses, including children's violent deaths, grief and the elderly and anticipatory grief. A series of vignettes, the best of the first and second editions plus new cases based on Worden's files, offer powerful training tools for the mental health

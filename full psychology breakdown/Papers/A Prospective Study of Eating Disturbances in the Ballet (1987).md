# A Prospective Study of Eating Disturbances in the Ballet

**Authors:** David M. Garner, Marion P. Olmsted
**Year:** 1987
**Citations:** 167
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1159/000288049

## Abstract

A group of 35 ballet students was assessed and then retested between 2 and 4 years later to determine the persistence of eating symptoms and to identify factors which predict eating disorders. Of those interviewed at follow-up, 25.7% had anorexia nervosa and 14.2% had either bulimia nervosa or a 'partial syndrome'. 'Drive for thinness' and 'body dissatisfaction' scales of the Eating Disorder Inventory were the only measures which predicted development of eating disorders at follow-up.

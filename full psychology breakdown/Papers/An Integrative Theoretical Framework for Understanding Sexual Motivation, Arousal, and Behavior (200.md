# An Integrative Theoretical Framework for Understanding Sexual Motivation, Arousal, and Behavior

**Authors:** Frederick Toates
**Year:** 2009
**Citations:** 333
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/00224490902747768

## Abstract

An integrative theoretical framework and model for understanding sexual motivation, arousal, and behavior is presented, combining the principles of incentive motivation theory and the hierarchical control of behavior. It is intended to stimulate discussion. The framework can serve as a "route map" in understanding the links between different component processes and their interactions, as well as the relations between different academic perspectives on understanding sexuality. It is suggested that both excitation and inhibition of sexual motivation, arousal, and behavior act at various levels in a hierarchical structure, and much confusion can be avoided by distinguishing these levels.

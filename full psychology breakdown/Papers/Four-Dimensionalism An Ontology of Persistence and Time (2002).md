# Four-Dimensionalism: An Ontology of Persistence and Time

**Authors:** Theodore Sider
**Year:** 2002
**Citations:** 852
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1093/019924443x.001.0001

## Abstract

Four-dimensionalism defends the thesis that the material world is composed of temporal as well as spatial parts. It addresses many topics concerning the metaphysics of time and identity over time.

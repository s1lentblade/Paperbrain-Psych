# The Effects of Family and Community Violence on Children

**Authors:** Gayla Margolin, Elana B. Gordis
**Year:** 2000
**Citations:** 1,227

## Abstract

This review examines theoretical and empirical literature on children's reactions to three types of violence--child maltreatment, community violence, and interparental violence. In addition to describing internalizing and externalizing problems associated with exposure to violence, this review identifies ways that violence can disrupt typical developmental trajectories through psychobiological effects, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD), cognitive consequences, and peer problems.

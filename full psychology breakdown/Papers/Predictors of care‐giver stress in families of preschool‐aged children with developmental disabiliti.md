# Predictors of care‐giver stress in families of preschool‐aged children with developmental disabilities

**Authors:** K. Plant, Matthew R. Sanders
**Year:** 2006
**Citations:** 362
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1365-2788.2006.00829.x

## Abstract

Difficulty of care-giving tasks, difficult child behaviour during care-giving tasks, and level of child disability are the primary factors which contribute to parent stress. Implications of these findings for future research and clinical practice are outlined.

# Personal resilience as a strategy for surviving and thriving in the face of workplace adversity: a literature review

**Authors:** Debra Jackson, Michel Edenborough
**Year:** 2007
**Citations:** 1,150
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1111/j.1365-2648.2007.04412.x

## Abstract

Our findings suggest that nurses can actively participate in the development and strengthening of their own personal resilience to reduce their vulnerability to workplace adversity and thus improve the overall healthcare setting. We recommend that resilience-building be incorporated into nursing education and that professional support should be encouraged through mentorship programmes outside nurses' immediate working environments.

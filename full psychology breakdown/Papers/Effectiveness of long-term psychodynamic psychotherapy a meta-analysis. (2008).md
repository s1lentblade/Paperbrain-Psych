# Effectiveness of long-term psychodynamic psychotherapy: a meta-analysis.

**Authors:** Falk Leichsenring, Sven Rabung
**Year:** 2008
**Citations:** 725
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/jama.300.13.1551

## Abstract

There is evidence that LTPP is an effective treatment for complex mental disorders. Further research should address the outcome of LTPP in specific mental disorders and should include cost-effectiveness analyses.

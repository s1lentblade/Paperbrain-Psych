# A meta-analytic review of achievement goal measures: Different labels for the same constructs or different constructs with similar labels?

**Authors:** Chris S. Hulleman, Judith M. Harackiewicz
**Year:** 2010
**Citations:** 1,283
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1037/a0018947

## Abstract

This meta-analysis addresses whether achievement goal researchers are using different labels for the same constructs or putting the same labels on different constructs. We systematically examined whether conceptual and methodological differences in the measurement of achievement goals moderated achievement goal intercorrelations and relationships with outcomes. We reviewed 243 correlational studies of self-reported achievement goals comprising a total of 91,087 participants.

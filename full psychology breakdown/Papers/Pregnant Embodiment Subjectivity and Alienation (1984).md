# Pregnant Embodiment: Subjectivity and Alienation

**Authors:** Iain M. Young
**Year:** 1984
**Citations:** 295
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1093/jmp/9.1.45

## Abstract

The pregnant subject has a unique experience of her body. The dichotomy between self and other, self and world, breaks down. She can experience a positive narcissism and sense of process. Some conceptualizations and practices of contemporary medicine, however, can alienate the pregnant subject from this bodily experience.

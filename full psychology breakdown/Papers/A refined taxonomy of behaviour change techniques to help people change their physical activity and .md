# A refined taxonomy of behaviour change techniques to help people change their physical activity and healthy eating behaviours: The CALO-RE taxonomy

**Authors:** Susan Michie, David French
**Year:** 2011
**Citations:** 1,819
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1080/08870446.2010.540664

## Abstract

This taxonomy can be used to improve the specification of interventions in published reports, thus improving replication, implementation and evidence syntheses. This will strengthen the scientific study of behaviour change and intervention development.

## Abstract

The «beautiful baby». The construction of bodily deviances by early childhood professionals This article is based on ethnographic research carried out in a Protection Maternelle et Infantile center. It attempts to analyze the way that early childhood professionals define «bad» parental education in order to transform it and shape it according to their own knowledge. The precise observation of children’s bodily signs which cause reactions of disapproval demonstrates three typical «figures déviantes» from which professionals organize their judgments.

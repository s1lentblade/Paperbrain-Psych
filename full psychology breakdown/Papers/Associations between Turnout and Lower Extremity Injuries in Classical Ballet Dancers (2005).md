# Associations between Turnout and Lower Extremity Injuries in Classical Ballet Dancers

**Authors:** Vicki Negus, N. Kathryn Briffa
**Year:** 2005
**Citations:** 150
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.2519/jospt.2005.35.5.307

## Abstract

Functional measures of turnout are more relevant than hip ER ROM to prevalence of nontraumatic dance injuries. Control of turnout in classical ballet dancers should be assessed dynamically and in functional positions.

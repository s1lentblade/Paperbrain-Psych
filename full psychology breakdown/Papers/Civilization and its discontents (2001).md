## Abstract

Abstract In the course of this book, I have argued that madness is not a mere aberration caused by some nasty quirk of current socio-economic or environmental conditions. Instead, the capacity for madness is a fundamental part of human nature. It is inherent in our psychological make-up, and preserved in the gene pool of our species, and for good reason. It is pre served because the very traits that make madness possible also underlie one of the things that we as humans most value, namely enhanced creativity.

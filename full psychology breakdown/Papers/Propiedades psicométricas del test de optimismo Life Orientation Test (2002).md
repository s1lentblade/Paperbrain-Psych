## Abstract

Psychometric properties of the Lif Orientation Test (LOT): An empirical application of the Multidie mensional Graded Response Model. This study examines the psychometric properties of the LOT-R Spanish adaptation using the m ultidimensional graded response model (MGRM). The study has two purposes: substantive and methodological. From a substantive point of view we want to assess the behaviour of a test which, in its original version, has interesting properties in applied personality measurement. From a methodological point of view our study illustrates the application of the MGRM in the personality domain, and allows us to assess whether the theoretical advantag es of the MGRM are important

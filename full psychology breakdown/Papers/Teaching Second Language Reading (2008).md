## Abstract

The book is an essential contribution to the already large reservoir of works on reading in the second language context. However, an initial response must surely come from the titling of the book. The word ‘teaching’ in the title may not be appropriate, as this book mainly leads readers (presumably the majority being teachers or trainee teachers) towards an understanding of reading processes (and of course all that is necessary in terms of a proper understanding of that phenomenon).

# Mental health and gender dysphoria: A review of the literature

**Authors:** Cecilia Dhejne, Jon Arcelus
**Year:** 2016
**Citations:** 563
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.3109/09540261.2015.1115753

## Abstract

Studies investigating the prevalence of psychiatric disorders among trans individuals have identified elevated rates of psychopathology. Research has also provided conflicting psychiatric outcomes following gender-confirming medical interventions. This review identifies 38 cross-sectional and longitudinal studies describing prevalence rates of psychiatric disorders and psychiatric outcomes, pre- and post-gender-confirming medical interventions, for people with gender dysphoria.

## Abstract

The functions of a manager in institutional responsability. The "action principles" and processes of inter-reaction manifested within institutional space are considered within a trans-subjective perspective aiming at articulating the caracteristics of the received subjects’ psychical workings with those of the medical group that will care for them. The article proposes to conceive of the "counter-action principles", the effects of symmetry and the splitting phenomena revealed in the clinic as the result of an induction generated by the sheer mass of projective identification deployed by the patients.

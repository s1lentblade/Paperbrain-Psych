# Trauma and PTSD Symptoms in Rwanda

**Authors:** Phuong Pham, Timothy Longman
**Year:** 2004
**Citations:** 361
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/jama.292.5.602

## Abstract

This study demonstrates that traumatic exposure, PTSD symptoms, and other factors are associated with attitudes toward justice and reconciliation. Societal interventions following mass violence should consider the effects of trauma if reconciliation is to be realized.

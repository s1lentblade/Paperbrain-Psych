# Understanding the Hispanic paradox.

**Authors:** Luisa Franzini, Arlene Keddie
**Year:** 2001
**Citations:** 744

## Abstract

Empirical studies should be conducted on the protective effects of immigrant status, identification with a subculture, interaction between acculturation and socioeconomic status, and supportive aspects of Hispanic culture.

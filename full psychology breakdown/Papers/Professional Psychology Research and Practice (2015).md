## Abstract

Abstract Professional Psychology: Research and Practice is a professional and scientific journal published by the American Psychological Association with a focus on the informational needs of the psychological practice community. The journal publishes articles of interest to independent practitioners and psychologists in institutional practice as well as educators and trainees. Literature reviews, surveys, empirical research, and policy pieces form the journal content, covering the science of psychology and integrating science into practice through evidence‐based practice concepts.

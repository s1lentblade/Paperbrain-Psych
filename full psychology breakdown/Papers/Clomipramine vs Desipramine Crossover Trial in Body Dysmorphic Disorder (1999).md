# Clomipramine vs Desipramine Crossover Trial in Body Dysmorphic Disorder

**Authors:** Eric Hollander, Daphne Simeon
**Year:** 1999
**Citations:** 275
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1001/archpsyc.56.11.1033

## Abstract

Clomipramine is more effective than desipramine in the treatment of body dysmorphic disorder and is effective even among those patients who are delusional.

# mHealth: A Mechanism to Deliver More Accessible, More Effective Mental Health Care

**Authors:** Matthew Price, Kenneth J. Ruggiero
**Year:** 2013
**Citations:** 538
**DOI:** https://doi.org/10.1002/cpp.1855

## Abstract

Mobile devices are becoming increasingly common among the adult population and have tremendous potential to advance clinical care. Mobile applications have the potential to enhance clinical care at stages of treatment-from engaging patients in clinical care to facilitating adherence to practices and in maintaining treatment gains. Research is needed to validate the efficacy and effectiveness of mobile applications in clinical practice. Research on such devices must incorporate assessments of usability and adherence in addition to their incremental benefit to treatment.
